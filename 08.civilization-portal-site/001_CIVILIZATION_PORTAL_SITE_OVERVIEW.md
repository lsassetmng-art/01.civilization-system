# CIVILIZATION PORTAL SITE OVERVIEW

## role
Civilization Portal Site is the official web/public entry system
for the civilization-system root.

## primary responsibilities
- official top-level entry
- official navigation across OS/app domains
- official discoverability and route presentation
- official public-facing access guidance
- public route packaging for approved destinations

## non-responsibilities
- not the canonical owner of PersonaOS truth
- not the canonical owner of BusinessOS truth
- not the canonical owner of LifeOS truth
- not the canonical owner of GameOS truth
- not the canonical owner of StreamingOS truth
- not the owner of application-internal runtime truth

## adjacent boundary
The portal site presents, routes, and exposes official entry points,
but domain truth remains owned by each respective OS or application owner.
