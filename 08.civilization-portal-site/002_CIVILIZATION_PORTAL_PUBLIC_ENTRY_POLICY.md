# CIVILIZATION PORTAL PUBLIC ENTRY POLICY

## 1. purpose
This document fixes what the portal may publicly expose.

## 2. portal-public eligible items
The portal may publicly expose:
- official destination pages
- official app entries
- official domain summaries
- approved category pages
- approved catalog/discovery pages
- approved campaign or event routes
- approved creator/game/stream/app discovery routes

## 3. portal-public ineligible items
The portal must not publicly expose:
- internal-only tools
- owner-private routes
- local-only assets
- review-pending routes without approval
- draft-only internal structures
- hidden implementation details that are not meant for public navigation

## 4. exposure levels
### level 1: public-discoverable
Officially discoverable from the portal.

### level 2: public-linkable
Publicly reachable by direct link, but not necessarily surfaced broadly.

### level 3: restricted
Not exposed on portal public navigation.

## 5. owner approval rule
Portal exposure should be based on owner-approved routes,
owner-approved labels, or clearly governed derived summaries.

## 6. wording rule
Portal wording should:
- orient users
- avoid claiming owner authority
- avoid redefining destination architecture
- avoid overpromising scope
