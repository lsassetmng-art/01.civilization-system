purpose:
Defines standard advisory consultation flow.

examples:
- ERP consulting
- management consulting
- medical consultation
- legal consultation
- improvement consultation

flow:
1. advisory app requests worker lending
2. foundation validates rank, specialty, world scope, and capacity
3. if required, disclaimer is presented
4. consent is collected and logged
5. advisory conversation session starts
6. advisory result may end as answer, escalation, or linked task
7. worker is released
