Defines the official overview of Business AI Worker.

summary:
Business AI Worker is the canonical BusinessOS layer
for company-scoped AI employment, assignment, rank,
subscription, slot capacity, reservation queue,
company work knowledge, company terminology,
usage logging, and authority boundary.

Persona-side trust, familiarity, affinity, and relationship-oriented
internal status remain outside Business AI Worker ownership
and belong to PersonaOS.

core_scope:
- AI employee employment
- AI employee assignment
- AI employee rank
- AI employee subscription
- AI employee slot capacity
- AI employee reservation queue
- company work knowledge
- company terminology
- usage log and audit
- authority boundary
- app consumption surface

rank_tiers:
- Entry: 1000 JPY
- Standard: 2000 JPY
- High: 3000 JPY
- Extra: 5000 JPY

capacity_rule:
AI employees are consumable by purchased employment capacity.
When all active slots are occupied,
new requests enter a reservation queue
until a slot becomes available
or additional capacity is subscribed.
