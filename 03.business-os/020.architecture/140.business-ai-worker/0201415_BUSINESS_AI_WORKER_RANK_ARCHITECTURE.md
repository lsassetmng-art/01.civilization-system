Basic assistance, template support, simple Q&A, low priority.

Standard:
Main practical worker, context-aware support, medium priority.

High:
High-quality support, complex case assistance, deep knowledge usage, high priority.

Extra:
Top-tier AI employee for normal work, advanced proposals,
forecasting assistance, management assistance, and improvement assistance.
Highest reservation priority.
