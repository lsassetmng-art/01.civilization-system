# BUSINESS PERSONA CONNECTION CANONICAL
# ============================================================

status: canonical
layer: integration
system: business-os

prepared_by: Zero

purpose:
Defines the canonical connection summary
between BusinessOS and PersonaOS.

# ============================================================
# 1. OFFICIAL BOUNDARY
# ============================================================

BusinessOS owns:
- company-scoped work knowledge
- company terminology
- AI worker employment
- AI worker subscription
- AI worker slots and reservation queue
- rank and capacity control
- lending, disclaimer, consent, audit, and usage control

PersonaOS owns:
- persona trust
- persona familiarity
- persona affinity
- reliability feeling
- relationship warmth
- other relationship-oriented internal status

# ============================================================
# 2. OFFICIAL CONNECTION RULE
# ============================================================

BusinessOS may emit official business-origin events
or business-origin effects.

PersonaOS validates those effects
and applies canonical internal-state results.

BusinessOS must not directly rewrite persona-side trust truth.

PersonaOS must not own company-scoped work knowledge,
worker slots, or subscription truth.

# ============================================================
# 3. AI WORKER RULE
# ============================================================

AI worker is governed by BusinessOS as a company-scoped work entity.

BusinessOS controls:
- worker lending
- reservation
- rank
- subscription
- specialty
- knowledge depth
- world scope
- activity mode
- disclaimer and consent
- execution and advisory governance

Consumer apps use worker capacity through BusinessOS foundation interfaces.

# ============================================================
# 4. PERSONA RULE
# ============================================================

PersonaOS receives official effects
from business-origin activities
and determines persona-side internal result canonically.

Business-side action may influence trust,
but PersonaOS determines the resulting trust state.

# ============================================================
# 5. OFFICIAL SUMMARY
# ============================================================

BusinessOS governs company work truth.
PersonaOS governs relationship-oriented persona truth.

Connection is effect-based and boundary-preserving.

amounts, display formats, and currency-aware processing

- Android
