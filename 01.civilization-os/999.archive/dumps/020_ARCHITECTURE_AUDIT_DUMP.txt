# ============================================================
# COMPANY ARCHITECTURE
# ============================================================

status: canonical
layer: architecture

definition:

Company is an economic organization operating inside a nation.

company_types:
- user_company
- ai_company

common_rules:
- both consume company cap
- total_company_count <= nation_company_cap
- founding conditions are equal for player and ai_human

founding_requirements:
- funding available
- achievement / score threshold
- empty company slot in nation

user_company:
- ceo must be player
- no auto dissolution
- can hire ai_human and ai_robot
- can participate in m&a / ipo / tob if eligible

ai_company:
- ceo is ai_human
- can auto dissolve
- treated as dynamic historical company entity

company_resources:
- national_currency
- labor
- assets
- reputation
- market_share

constraints:
- ai_robot cannot be ceo
- user company ceo cannot be ai_human

# ============================================================
# TAX ARCHITECTURE
# ============================================================

status: canonical
layer: architecture

definition:

Tax is imposed by nation and platform according to separate layers.

tax_types:
- corporate_tax
- platform_fee
- wage_tax (future extension)
- transaction_tax (future extension)

corporate_tax:
- paid in national_currency
- based on nation-defined tax model
- may use score-based or law-defined structure

platform_fee:
- applied at monthly settlement
- base_rate: 30%
- reduced_rate: 20% when condition is satisfied
- no fee above defined platform fee model

principles:
- state tax is legal layer
- platform fee is system settlement layer
- both are separate
