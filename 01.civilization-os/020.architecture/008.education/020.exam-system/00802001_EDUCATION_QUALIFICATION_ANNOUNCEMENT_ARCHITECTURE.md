# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for school admission,
qualification examination access,
marketplace distribution,
public announcement,
and personal alerting
inside Civilization.

This architecture defines:

- school admission structure
- qualification structure
- marketplace application forms
- marketplace examination tickets
- announcement behavior
- personal alert behavior
- life-course relevance to Persona progression


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Education and qualification systems
must not remain hidden market items only.

Because they directly affect
Persona life progression,
career access,
public office access,
and social mobility,
they must be visible through:

- formal public announcements
- marketplace distribution
- personal alerting
- career and life progression surfaces


# ============================================================
# 3. SCHOOL AND QUALIFICATION SEPARATION
# ============================================================

School admissions and qualification exams
must remain separate.

Schools use:

- application forms

Qualifications use:

- examination tickets

This separation must remain visible
in market, UI, and system rules.


# ============================================================
# 4. CURRENCY PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

All education and qualification related costs
must be payable by:

- national_currency
- civ_cash

CIV_CASH is universally applicable.

National currency remains
the domestic standard baseline.


# ============================================================
# 5. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Education and qualification opportunity
must behave as a visible life system,
not as a hidden backend mechanic.
