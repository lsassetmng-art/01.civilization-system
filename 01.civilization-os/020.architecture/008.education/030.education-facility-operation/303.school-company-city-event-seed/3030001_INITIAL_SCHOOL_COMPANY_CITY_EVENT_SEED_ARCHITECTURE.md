# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for school events,
company events,
city events,
festival-linked public events,
and recurring institutional annual cycles
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

School, company, and city events must reflect:
- nation identity
- calendar and holiday posture
- school culture
- labor and hiring culture
- port, market, or industrial identity
- religion and memorial culture
- civic scale and urban symbolism


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- school annual event posture
- company annual event posture
- city and district event posture
- hiring-season and graduation-season effects
- links to religious or national calendar


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

School, company, and city event seed must make visible
how institutions animate the calendar year.
