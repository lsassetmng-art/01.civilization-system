# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical compatibility architecture
between Civilization actor classes
and public office classes.

This architecture extends civil service eligibility
into broader office domains:

- political office
- military office
- judicial office
- ceremonial office
- administrative office
- support execution role


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Actor compatibility must be resolved
through three layers:

- lifeform classification
- office class requirement
- nation-specific office rule

This prevents confusion between:

- being able to perform a task
- being able to hold an office
- being able to inherit or legally occupy authority


# ============================================================
# 3. HUMAN CATEGORY RULE
# ============================================================

Human category actors:
- user_persona
- ai_human

Non-human category actors:
- ai_robot

Ai_robot may perform support roles,
but must not hold offices
that explicitly require human status.


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Public office compatibility
must remain office-sensitive,
nation-sensitive,
and actor-class explicit.
