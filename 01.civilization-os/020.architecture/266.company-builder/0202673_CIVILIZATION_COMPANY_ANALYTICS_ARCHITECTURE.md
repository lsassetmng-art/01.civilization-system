purpose:
Define the official company analytics architecture
inside CivilizationOS.

This architecture covers:
- company metrics
- growth analysis
- site analysis
- product/portfolio analysis
- company comparison
- trend and readiness views


# ============================================================
# 1. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Company analytics must distinguish:
- current state metrics
- historical trend metrics
- comparative metrics
- qualitative score metrics
- event-driven changes

Core principle:

analytics is a structured interpretation of company state,
not merely a copy of raw records


# ============================================================
# 2. ANALYTICS DOMAINS
# ============================================================

Minimum analytics domains:
- sales and revenue
- product or portfolio performance
- site/store performance
- growth and reputation
- company evaluation score
- public/company visibility
- listing-readiness score where applicable


# ============================================================
# 3. FINAL ARCHITECTURAL RULE
# ============================================================

Company analytics in CivilizationOS must be:
- metric-aware
- trend-aware
- comparable
- evaluation-aware
