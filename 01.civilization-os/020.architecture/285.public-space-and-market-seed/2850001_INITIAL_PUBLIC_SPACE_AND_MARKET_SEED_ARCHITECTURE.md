# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for initial public spaces, squares, memorial facilities,
and market facilities in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Public spaces and markets must reflect:
- nation identity
- city role
- civic life
- ritual and memorial culture
- trade posture
- class history
- tourism and festival structure

These facilities are core urban meaning structures,
not decoration only.


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- park naming posture
- square naming posture
- memorial naming posture
- market naming posture
- city-level assignment of public spaces and market facilities


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Public-space and market seed must make each city's
civic and symbolic life legible.
