# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Define the canonical architecture
for avoidance,
mitigation,
recovery,
legal-process resolution,
and service-based recovery
for User and Ai(Human)
inside Civilization.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Autonomous progression
must allow intervention and recovery.

Users may intervene through:

- items
- company services
- public administrative services
- lawful currency-based actions

Ai(Human) may also use:

- company services
- public administrative services
- items or item-equivalent recovery means where allowed
- lawful currency-based actions

The primary structural difference is:

- user may perform external paid charging
- Ai(Human) may not perform external paid charging


# ============================================================
# 3. AVOIDANCE AND RECOVERY FAMILIES
# ============================================================

Representative intervention families include:

- avoidance before negative outcome
- mitigation after negative outcome
- health recovery
- mental recovery
- schedule recovery
- legal-process resolution
- social or institutional support use


# ============================================================
# 4. ITEM RELATION
# ============================================================

Users may use items for:

- avoidance
- mitigation
- recovery

Representative outcomes include:

- avoiding abnormal outcome
- reducing incident impact
- recovering health or stability
- improving schedule posture


# ============================================================
# 5. SERVICE RELATION
# ============================================================

User and Ai(Human) may use:

- company services
- public administrative services
- judicial or lawful process services
- health and support services

These services may affect:

- recovery
- legal release
- protection
- stabilization
- support access


# ============================================================
# 6. BAIL AND LAWFUL CURRENCY RELATION
# ============================================================

Lawful release mechanisms such as bail
may use national currency.

This must remain distinct from:

- external paid charging
- Marketplace payment
- CIV_CASH-only flows

National-currency legal actions
belong to state and public-process posture.


# ============================================================
# 7. USER VS AI(HUMAN) DIFFERENCE
# ============================================================

Except for external paid charging capability,
Ai(Human) should remain structurally compatible
with user-side recovery and intervention routes.

Ai(Human) recovery and legal-process outcomes
must still depend on:

- service access
- lawful currency
- asset posture
- institutional access
- actor condition


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

User and Ai(Human) intervention and recovery architecture must remain:

- recovery-capable
- avoidance-capable
- service-aware
- legal-process-aware
- national-currency-aware
- explicit in user vs Ai(Human) charging difference
