# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for war,
mobilization,
alliance,
diplomatic pressure,
and interstate conflict play
inside Civilization.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Military play and diplomacy play
must remain connected.

War does not begin in isolation.

It emerges through:

- authority decision
- diplomatic deterioration
- alliance commitments
- mobilization posture
- domestic legitimacy
- logistical capability


# ============================================================
# 3. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Military and diplomacy play
must connect state decision,
force posture,
and cross-border consequence.
