# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for assigning anchor companies to major seed-world cities.

This layer maps:
- nation anchor company sets
- city roles
- city industry posture
- system service access
into explicit city-level anchor company presence.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Anchor company assignment must reflect:
- nation identity
- city function
- transport and logistics structure
- industrial concentration
- housing and population support
- symbolic capital role

This layer defines structured presence,
not full parcel-by-parcel placement.


# ============================================================
# 3. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Each major city should have explicit anchor companies
that explain why the city exists and how it functions.
