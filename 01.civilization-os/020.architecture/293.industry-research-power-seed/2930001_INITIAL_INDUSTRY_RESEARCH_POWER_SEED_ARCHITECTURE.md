Defines the canonical architecture
for initial industrial facilities,
research facilities,
power-generation facilities,
resource-processing facilities,
and industrial-cluster placement
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Industrial, research, and power facilities must reflect:
- nation identity
- city role
- energy posture
- transport and logistics structure
- labor and education structure
- military exposure if relevant
- technological ideology and limits


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- industrial naming posture
- research naming posture
- power naming posture
- city-level assignment
- special technology restrictions if applicable


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Industry, research, and power seed
must make visible how production, innovation,
and energy actually function in the world.
