# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for personal ascent,
career expansion,
fall from power,
and social collapse play
inside Civilization.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Civilization play must support
small beginnings and large consequences.

A user may rise through:

- education
- business
- military
- political office
- media influence
- faction support

A user may fall through:

- bankruptcy
- scandal
- defeat
- arrest
- purge
- exile
- execution_path_if_allowed_by nation rules


# ============================================================
# 3. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Personal play must connect
individual trajectory
to world-scale consequence.
