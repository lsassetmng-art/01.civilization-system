# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This template defines a commerce-oriented nation preset.

It is designed for nations that prioritize:

- trade
- markets
- company activity
- retail circulation
- office concentration
- finance and securities development

This template is suitable for nations that aim to become
high-activity economic hubs.
