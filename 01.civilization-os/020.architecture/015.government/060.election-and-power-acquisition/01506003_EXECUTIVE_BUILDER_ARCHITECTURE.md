# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for Executive Builder
inside Nation Builder and Power Structure Builder.

This builder defines:

- head of state structure
- head of government structure
- ministry structure
- appointment authority
- decree authority
- emergency authority
- command hierarchy
- practical executive control distribution


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Executive Builder must separate:

- formal executive structure
- legal executive powers
- practical command distribution
- emergency escalation
- informal real-control channels

A state may have a symbolic ruler
with strong informal pressure.
A state may also have a formal cabinet
that is operationally weaker
than command, religion, finance, or bureaucracy.


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED SUBSYSTEMS
# ============================================================

The builder must include:

- Head of State Builder
- Head of Government Builder
- Executive Office Builder
- Ministry Structure Builder
- Appointment Authority Builder
- Decree and Order Builder
- Emergency Power Builder
- Command Chain Builder
- Executive Validation Engine

# ============================================================

The Executive Builder must support

commercial executives,
command states,
religious rule,
weak federative administrations,
and unstable reconstruction executives
without confusing office form
with real control.
