# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical binding architecture
between:

- civil service eligibility
- public office actor compatibility
- political power play
- military and diplomacy play
- grand play UI integration

This architecture defines how office eligibility
becomes visible and actionable
inside Civilization play surfaces.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Office eligibility must not remain
a hidden background rule.

It must be visible through:

- political ascent surfaces
- military promotion surfaces
- office eligibility surfaces
- nation detail surfaces
- personal profile surfaces
- grand play summary surfaces


# ============================================================
# 3. SEPARATION PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

The following must remain distinct:

- actor class
- human category
- office class
- office type
- entry route
- authority scope
- support execution role

A user must be able to understand
why an actor may perform tasks
without being legally eligible
for a human-only office.


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Office eligibility binding
must preserve legal clarity,
nation difference,
and actor-class difference
without hiding playable progression.
