# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for romance culture,
marriage values,
family values,
child-raising values,
and relationship psychology
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Family culture and relationship psychology must reflect:
- nation identity
- religion posture
- labor and household structure
- class structure
- housing access
- childcare posture
- eldercare posture
- civic psychology and value axes

These are not abstract morals only.
They explain what people think love is,
why marriage happens or is delayed,
what counts as family duty,
and what child-raising means.


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation seed should define:
- romance posture
- marriage posture
- divorce tolerance posture
- family duty posture
- child-raising posture
- household independence posture
- kinship obligation posture
- class influence on family posture


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Family culture seed must make visible
how people form bonds,
justify duty,
and interpret adulthood and parenthood.
