# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical naming architecture
for initial anchor companies in Civilization seed-world design.

This layer fixes naming culture by nation
before detailed company-by-city assignment.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Anchor company naming must reflect:
- nation history
- political culture
- industrial identity
- city and regional tradition
- symbolic naming posture

Naming is not random flavor text.
It is part of nation identity design.


# ============================================================
# 3. NATION NAMING POSTURES
# ============================================================

helios_democratic_kingdom:
- large companies often use "Helios"
- kingdom-era symbolic legacy remains visible

nova_commercial_federation:
- merchant-origin, commercial, financial, international naming
- ASIC remains as-is

seiwa_state:
- nation name, city name, ordinary surnames
- practical and credible naming posture
- Ninomiya naming is reserved for the railway company only

gladia_military_alliance:
- American-style English naming
- defense, mobility, force, and contract-security tone

orpheus_oceanic_union:
- founder names must be avoided
- island, port, sea, tide, ritual, brewing, fishery naming posture

free_cities_union:
- surname-based merchant, workshop, port, guild naming posture

aurelia_federal_republic:
- names praising the nation or founder
- state, doctrinal, or old-regime naming tone


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Initial anchor company naming is a nation-specific
seed-world identity layer.
