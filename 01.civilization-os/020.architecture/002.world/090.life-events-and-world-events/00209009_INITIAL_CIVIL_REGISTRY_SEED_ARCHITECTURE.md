# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for civil registry, family registry,
birth and death registration,
marriage registration,
inheritance registration,
and funeral-linked civil institutions
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Civil registry facilities must reflect:
- nation identity
- family structure
- religion and memorial posture
- legal and civic procedure culture
- education and welfare linkage
- urban and rural accessibility

These facilities are core institutions
that connect life events to legal reality.


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- birth registry facilities
- marriage registry facilities
- death registry facilities
- family and citizen registry facilities
- inheritance registry facilities
- funeral and memorial civil support facilities
- city-level assignment


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Civil registry seed must make visible
how life events become recognized, recorded,
and socially processed in each nation.
