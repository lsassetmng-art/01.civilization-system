# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for shopping behavior,
market-use behavior,
price sensitivity,
brand sensitivity,
second-hand acceptance,
and everyday consumer decision culture
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Consumer behavior and market culture must reflect:
- nation identity
- class structure
- household burden
- food and nightlife culture
- market openness
- luxury visibility
- poverty and savings pressure
- trust in brands and sellers


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation seed should define:
- shopping frequency posture
- price sensitivity posture
- brand sensitivity posture
- bargaining posture
- second-hand acceptance posture
- market versus store posture
- class-linked consumer gap posture


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Consumer-behavior seed must make visible
how citizens choose, compare, save, show status,
and survive through daily buying decisions.
