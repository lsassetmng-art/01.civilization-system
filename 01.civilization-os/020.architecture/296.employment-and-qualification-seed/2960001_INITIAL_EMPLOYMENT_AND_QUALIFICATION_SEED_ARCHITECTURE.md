# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for employment offices,
job-matching facilities,
career support facilities,
qualification testing facilities,
and vocational transition facilities
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Employment and qualification facilities must reflect:
- nation identity
- labor structure
- education posture
- industry posture
- household structure
- youth transition structure
- public and private hiring culture

For Helios specifically:
- historical AI aptitude-based occupational assignment must remain visible
- Helios-linked firms often continue to hire lower and middle strata
  based on aptitude judgment outcomes
- noble-family business succession remains structurally separate
  from aptitude-based hiring logic

For Gladia specifically:
- compulsory service must remain visible as a major labor-market institution
- eligible citizens enter a three-year service period between ages 25 and 28
- rear-support military deployment may occur during compulsory service
- front-line deployment is prohibited during compulsory service
- employer seat preservation, family support, income guarantee,
  substitute employment support, compensation, and return-to-work retraining
  are part of the labor-system design


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- employment naming posture
- qualification naming posture
- vocational transition naming posture
- internship and interview support posture
- city-level assignment


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Employment seed must make visible
how students become workers,
how workers change roles,
and how qualifications shape social mobility.
