# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for initial courts, legal institutions,
contract enforcement facilities,
and dispute-resolution facilities
in Civilization seed-world planning.


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE RULE
# ============================================================

Legal and judicial facilities must reflect:
- nation identity
- state legitimacy model
- trade and contract posture
- public order philosophy
- ideological or doctrinal influence
- military or civic control structure

These facilities are core institutions
that determine how rules become reality.


# ============================================================
# 3. REQUIRED DIMENSIONS
# ============================================================

Each nation and city seed should define:
- court naming posture
- contract enforcement posture
- arbitration and mediation posture
- city-level legal facility assignment
- special legal distortions if applicable


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Legal and judicial seed must make visible
how each nation resolves conflict,
enforces contracts, and legitimizes authority.
