# PURPOSE

Define the canonical architecture
for streamer agencies inside Civilization.

A streamer agency is a Civilization company-side structure
that supports:

- talent scouting
- streamer management
- Civilization-side announcement
- ordinary goods sales through Civilization Marketplace
- physical fulfillment for agency-related physical goods
- revenue mirror intake from StreamingOS


# POSITION

Streamer agency belongs to Civilization company structures.

Streamer agency is not StreamingOS truth.

StreamingOS remains the source of truth for:

- channel
- video posting
- stream session
- live event
- event ticket sales
- live-event limited sale
- event video sales


# CIVILIZATION RESPONSIBILITIES

Streamer agency is responsible for:

- agency company profile
- talent scouting record
- roster management
- Civilization announcement execution
- Civilization Marketplace ordinary goods sales
- physical goods fulfillment where applicable
- mirrored revenue intake as Civilization sales numbers


# SCOUTING PRINCIPLE

Streamer agency may scout:

- user
- ai_human
- ai_robot

Scouting eligibility and event-role eligibility
must be separated.

Being scouted does not automatically grant:

- organizer authority
- audience authority
- performer authority


# ANNOUNCEMENT PRINCIPLE

Civilization-side announcements
must be executed by the streamer agency.

Streaming events do not announce directly into Civilization.

The canonical route is:

Streaming-side promotion need
-> streamer agency request
-> Civilization announcement execution


# ORDINARY GOODS PRINCIPLE

Ordinary streamer goods
must be sold through Civilization Marketplace.

These goods are normal marketplace goods,
not live-event limited sale goods.


# FULFILLMENT PRINCIPLE

Physical fulfillment is allowed only for
streamer-agency-related sales.

This includes:

- Civilization Marketplace ordinary physical goods
- StreamingOS live-event limited sale physical goods
  when the seller is tied to a streamer agency

Digital products are not fulfillment targets.


# MIRROR PRINCIPLE

StreamingOS revenue truth remains in StreamingOS.

Civilization receives only mirrored finalized sales numbers
where applicable.


# FINAL RULE

Streamer agency
must act as the Civilization-side company and logistics bridge
for streaming-linked commerce.
