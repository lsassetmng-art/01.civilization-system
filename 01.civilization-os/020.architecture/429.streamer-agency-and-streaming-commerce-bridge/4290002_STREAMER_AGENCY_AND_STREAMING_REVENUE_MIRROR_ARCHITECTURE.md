# PURPOSE

Define how StreamingOS revenue
is mirrored into Civilization sales
without transferring source-of-truth ownership.


# SOURCE OF TRUTH

StreamingOS is the source of truth for:

- channel revenue
- live event revenue
- event ticket revenue
- event limited sale revenue
- event video sales revenue

Civilization is not allowed to overwrite
StreamingOS revenue truth.


# CIVILIZATION MIRROR SCOPE

Civilization may receive only:

- finalized revenue numbers
- settlement-ready summarized values
- company-level mirrored revenue projections where allowed

Civilization must not become
the primary transaction ledger for StreamingOS sales.


# MIRROR TARGET

Mirrored numbers may be added to:

- streamer agency sales
- company revenue summaries
- nation-level economic effects where allowed


# MIRROR TIMING

Mirror intake occurs only after
the Streaming-side source state becomes finalized
or settlement-ready according to Streaming rules.


# FINAL RULE

Revenue truth stays in StreamingOS.
Civilization may mirror finalized numbers only.
