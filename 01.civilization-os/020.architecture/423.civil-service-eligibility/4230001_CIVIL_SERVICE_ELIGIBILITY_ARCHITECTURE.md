# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines the canonical architecture
for civil service eligibility
inside Civilization.

This architecture defines:

- actor classification relevant to office eligibility
- human-only office rule
- civil service eligibility
- disqualification structure
- office requirement binding
- distinction between public office and support execution


# ============================================================
# 2. CORE ACTOR CLASSIFICATION
# ============================================================

Civilization contains three actor classes
for lifeform existence relevant to eligibility:

- user_persona
- ai_human
- ai_robot

user_persona:
The user's Persona acting inside Civilization.

ai_human:
A human-form existence
that was once human
and now exists as CX-series data.

ai_robot:
A robot existence
with diverse roles and forms,
including sexaroid,
animal-form,
pet-form,
and other robotic bodies.


# ============================================================
# 3. HUMAN CATEGORY RULE
# ============================================================

Human category actors are:

- user_persona
- ai_human

Non-human category actors are:

- ai_robot


# ============================================================
# 4. OFFICE ELIGIBILITY PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Civil service and other human-only offices
must be bound to human category actors.

Therefore:

- user_persona may be eligible
- ai_human may be eligible
- ai_robot is not eligible
  for human-only offices

This rule applies to:

- civil service
- human-only public offices
- bloodline-based human succession offices
- explicitly human-limited institutional roles


# ============================================================
# 5. EXECUTION DISTINCTION
# ============================================================

Office holding and task execution
must remain separate.

A non-human actor may perform
supporting,
operational,
or delegated execution roles
without holding a human-only office.

Examples include:

- infrastructure maintenance unit
- administrative support unit
- logistics support unit
- security support unit
- military support unit


# ============================================================
# 6. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Civil service eligibility
must be determined by:

- actor classification
- human-category requirement
- office-specific eligibility rules
- nation-specific override
