purpose:
Define generic policy for the finance-credit support layer
inside CivilizationOS.

This layer exists to support:
- financial obligations
- insurance
- subsidy
- relief
- generic claims

It is not the detailed policy source of truth for:
- banking services
- mortgage finance
- enterprise lending


# ============================================================
# 1. CORE POLICY
# ============================================================

The finance-credit support layer must remain:
- generic
- cross-domain
- non-duplicative of banking
- non-duplicative of real-estate finance

Detailed lending and repayment policies must live
in their specialized domains.


# ============================================================
# 2. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

170.finance-credit is a generic support layer,
not the canonical detailed lending layer.
