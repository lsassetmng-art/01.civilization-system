# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

posture:
- funeral quality and memorial scale remain class-sensitive
- weak households may experience unstable post-death transition
- upper strata preserve estate and business continuity strongly
- family-business inheritance is especially visible among noble-origin households
- lower strata often face property fragility and weaker institutional buffering


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

posture:
- inheritance is strongly tied to asset management and household wealth continuity
- legal and commercial estate processing is highly visible
- premium memorial and premium estate management exist
- family continuity is often filtered through asset and business value


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

posture:
- death, funeral, inheritance, and registration are orderly and procedural
- family continuity is stable and institutionally buffered
- household transition after death is comparatively predictable
- legal and administrative inheritance handling is strong


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

posture:
- service death, honor death, and controlled memorial structure are visible
- family support after death is institutionally strong
- veteran and service lineage continuity matter
- state honor and household stabilization coexist


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

posture:
- mourning is family, island, and ritual centered
- funeral and remembrance are culturally rich and community-linked
- inheritance may be informal, kinship-heavy, and household-embedded
- craft, boat, fishery, and island asset succession can be relational rather than heavily bureaucratic


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

posture:
- death is filtered through doctrine, state oversight, and instability
- state influence over succession can be strong
- household continuity may depend on political or doctrinal position
- memorial practice can be ideologically charged


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

posture:
- city-specific variation is high
- some cities use structured civic inheritance handling
- some cities rely on family, guild, and community settlement
- small-business and workshop succession are especially visible


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

End-of-life and succession posture must remain nation-specific
so that property, grief, and continuity feel socially distinct.
