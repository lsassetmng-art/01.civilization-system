# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- class-layered access and school quality differences must remain expressible
- elite and upper-track schools should diverge strongly from outer-district schools
- aptitude filtering and status reproduction should remain visible
- discipline, prestige, and patron influence may overlap


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- economics, management, commercial skill, and performance visibility should be strong
- business presentation, competition, and practical utility should be emphasized
- premium educational tracks should diverge clearly from ordinary urban tracks


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- procedural order, school stability, exam structure, and legal-administrative education should be strongest
- guidance, institutional continuity, and public legitimacy should be emphasized
- school should feel like a national backbone


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- discipline, service suitability, physical readiness, and orderly hierarchy should be supported
- military-adjacent education routes should be visually and structurally legible
- spontaneity should be weaker than organized form


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- arts, music, dance, craft, fishery, and communal island education must be strongly supported
- family and community participation should remain visible
- formal hierarchy should often feel weaker than communal continuity


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- doctrine filtering, instability, and fragile schooling conditions should be expressible
- protected and unprotected student experiences may diverge sharply
- schooling may feel controlled, constrained, or uneven


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- strong city-by-city differences must be supported
- guild-linked, civic, port, and workshop-oriented schooling may diverge heavily
- local identity should often override federal identity


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Education UI must visibly change by country and city,
not only by school level.
