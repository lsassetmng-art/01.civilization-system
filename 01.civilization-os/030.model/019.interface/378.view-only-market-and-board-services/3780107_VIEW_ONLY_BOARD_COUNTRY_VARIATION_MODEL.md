# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- prestige indicators and elite-favored signals may be highlighted
- information visibility may be uneven by status or district
- finance boards may feel more power-marked than neutral


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- graph-heavy, ranking-heavy, and performance-heavy display should be strongest
- premium and speculative market visibility should be high
- comparison tools should be emphasized


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- orderly, legible, stable information boards should be strongest
- practical and civic usability should outweigh hype
- official notice surfaces should be clear and structured


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- strategic and practical indicators should be emphasized over speculative display
- overly decorative financial presentation should be weaker
- civic or state utility should be clearer


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- local and regional board tone should be warmer and more community-linked
- smaller-scale boards may feel more natural than massive market halls
- comparison may lean local rather than global


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- stressed, incomplete, or politically filtered information visibility must be expressible
- open trust in boards may be weaker
- notices and restrictions may dominate the surface


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city board culture should vary strongly
- port-city finance boards and local trade boards may differ sharply
- local regulation should visibly affect board structure


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

View-only boards must visibly differ by country
in tone, trust, and information emphasis.
