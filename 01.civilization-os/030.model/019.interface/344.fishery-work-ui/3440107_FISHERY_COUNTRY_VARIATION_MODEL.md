# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- fishery may exist but should feel less identity-central than industry or urban supply
- district inequality and market access differences should still be expressible
- prestige seafood channels may diverge from ordinary supply chains


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- premium seafood, fast market timing, and price optimization should be emphasized
- logistics and freshness arbitrage should be strong
- commercial scaling and brand logic may matter more than communal fishery identity


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- stable domestic supply, orderly distribution, and school/family food-chain linkage should be supported
- fishery should connect strongly to reliable transport and food planning
- practical value should outweigh spectacle


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- fishery should feel secondary to strategic logistics unless regionally specific
- preservation, ration suitability, and durable supply relevance may matter
- military or state reserve logic may affect distribution


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- this must be the strongest and most identity-central fishery model
- island livelihood, communal trust, harbor rhythm, ritual use, and family continuity must all be highly visible
- weather, ferry linkage, and local reputation should deeply shape play


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- fragile harbor conditions, unstable preservation, and survival supply logic should be expressible
- local scarcity and damaged logistics may dominate fishery value
- recovery and loss should feel harsher


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- port-city and harbor-city variation should be strong
- some cities may run highly commercial fishery, others local market fishery
- local rules and market ecology should override broad federation uniformity


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Fishery UI must visibly change by country and harbor ecology,
not only by vessel size.
