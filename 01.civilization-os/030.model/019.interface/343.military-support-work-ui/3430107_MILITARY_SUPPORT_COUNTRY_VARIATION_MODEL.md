# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- internal inequality and patron influence must remain expressible
- some units may receive preferential support through rank or influence
- worker and outer-district support strain may be harsher
- discipline may be uneven compared with Gladia


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- contract efficiency, procurement visibility, and cost-performance logic should be strong
- premium units and ordinary units may diverge in support quality
- logistics optimization and outsourcing-style logic should be supportable


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- structured, reliable, and procedurally clear support logic should be strongest
- rail-linked support and administrative traceability should be emphasized
- compensation and reintegration flow should feel stable and orderly


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- this must be the strongest and most central military support model
- readiness, command priority, conscription support, family support, and seat protection must all be highly visible
- rear support should feel like a core national system, not a minor subsystem


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- military support should generally feel lighter and more local or maritime
- island transport and harbor logistics may matter more than heavy land-chain systems
- communal support logic may appear in some personnel recovery paths


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- unstable supply, ideological filtering, and fragile support continuity should be expressible
- protected units and neglected units may diverge sharply
- compensation and reintegration may feel unreliable


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city and contract-by-contract variation should be supported
- unified doctrine is weaker than negotiated practical support logic
- port and civic logistics may matter more than centralized military chain identity


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Military support UI must visibly change by country,
especially in the meaning of readiness,
fairness, and recovery.
