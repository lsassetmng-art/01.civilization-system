# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- elite-facing and worker-facing facilities may diverge strongly
- prestige storefronts and rougher district services should coexist
- finance access may visibly reflect status differences


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- commercial density, premium storefronts, and branded finance services should be strongest
- securities and direct store visibility should be high
- mixed-use commercial towers should be common


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- clear separation between official services, practical services, and civic boards should be strongest
- market board and public information facilities should feel orderly
- transport-linked service hubs should be strong


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- practical and institutional facilities should dominate
- speculative or decorative storefront emphasis should be weaker
- official and disciplined service routing should be strong


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- local markets, communal storefronts, ritual facilities, and island service hubs should be visible
- smaller mixed-use buildings may be more natural than large towers
- local trust may matter more than formal branding


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- public and private service access may be unstable or uneven
- protected and unprotected service facilities may diverge sharply
- view-only boards may expose stressed markets rather than orderly ones


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-specific facility ecology must be strong
- port finance, guild storefronts, civic boards, and private direct stores may all vary heavily
- local regulation should visibly alter facility mix


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Facility catalog variation must explain
why the same service category
looks and routes differently by country.
