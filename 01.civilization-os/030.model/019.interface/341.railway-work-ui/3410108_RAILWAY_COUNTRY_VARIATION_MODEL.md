# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- route inequality and district access difference should remain expressible
- upper and lower district service quality may diverge
- industrial and worker logistics importance should remain visible
- informal influence may distort service priority


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- urban speed, business time value, and dense-transfer logic should be emphasized
- premium commuter and executive service patterns may diverge sharply
- profitability and convenience visibility should be strong


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- strongest rail-civilization identity must be visible
- punctuality, timetable trust, civic integration, and long-distance normalization should dominate
- rail should feel like national infrastructure, not just transport business


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- military support rail, strategic route discipline, and command priority should be expressible
- freight, troop, and strategic movement may override ordinary passenger comfort
- disruption response should feel more command-driven


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- rail importance should generally be lower than ferry and harbor connectivity
- limited or local rail logic may exist, but island linkage should dominate mobility meaning
- weather and transfer to sea routes should matter strongly


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- instability, undermaintenance, and uneven service reliability should be supportable
- reconstruction lines and fragile operation should be visible
- state priority may override ordinary schedule stability


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-to-city rail logic must vary strongly
- some cities may be transit-rich, others port-first or mixed-modal
- local political bargaining may shape route and station priority


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Railway UI must visibly change by country and city,
not only by line size or train class.
