# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- White Home scale, visibility, and elite patronage must be strongly supported
- religion may mix salvation, political influence, and public legitimacy
- grand ritual and major patron donation logic should be visible


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- religion is generally weaker as a central daily system
- premium spiritual services, niche ritual markets, or reputation-driven patronage may exist
- commercial presentation pressure may shape visible religious operations


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- orderly memorial, civic-compatible ritual, and stable community religious operation should be emphasized
- religion should often feel integrated with family duty and public order rather than raw political capture


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- state and service ritual may matter more than expansive everyday pluralistic religion
- military memorial and disciplined ceremonial logic should be supportable
- overt doctrine-market behavior should be weaker than in Helios or Aurelia


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- communal ritual, island festivals, local sacred sites, and music-linked belief expression must be highly visible
- pilgrimage may be local and communal rather than only monumental
- ritual warmth and community integration should be strong


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- doctrine-heavy, fear-filtered, and politically captured religion must be strongly expressible
- religious work may overlap with ideological supervision
- insiders and outsiders should experience religious institutions differently


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city religious ecology must be supported
- some cities may be secular-civic, others guild-religious, others port-syncretic
- local sacred identity should often override federation tone


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Religion UI must visibly change by country and city,
not only by denomination scale.
