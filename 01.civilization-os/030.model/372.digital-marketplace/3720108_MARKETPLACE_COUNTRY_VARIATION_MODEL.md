# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- prestige-heavy and rough-market coexistence must be supportable
- elite-recognized branding may gain visible advantage
- sensitive listings may be filtered unevenly by power influence


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- strongest commercial and premium ranking culture
- branding, velocity, review influence, and ad visibility should be strongest
- premium digital packs should stand out heavily


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- safety, trust, practicality, and official clarity should be strongest
- education, ceremony, and household-use digital goods should be stable categories
- imitation sensitivity should be high


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- practical and institutional digital goods should be more central than decorative excess
- state-sensitive restrictions may be stronger
- discipline and official-use categories may have elevated visibility


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- ritual, music, local culture, and communal festival packs should be highly visible
- local identity and craft-like digital presentation should matter strongly
- communal warmth should outweigh premium polish in many cases


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- doctrine-sensitive restrictions and visibility filtering must be strongly expressible
- open market trust should feel weaker
- public availability and hidden restriction may diverge sharply


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city platform culture must be supported
- guild-like storefront logic, port-city trends, and civic variation may all differ
- local reputation may shape listing visibility strongly


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Marketplace must visibly differ by nation and city,
not only by product type.
