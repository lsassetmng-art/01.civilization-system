# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

posture:
- school access remains historically class-layered
- commoner compulsory route exists
- outer districts gained later compulsory access
- noble and upper tracks still convert education into status more efficiently
- AI-industrial and technical routes remain prestigious
- aptitude and class history shape later branching strongly


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

posture:
- progression strongly rewards skill, performance, and market usefulness
- management, economics, commerce, and enterprise routes are thick
- technical routes remain strong in ASIC-linked tracks
- exam culture exists but practical results and later earning potential matter more than pure credentialism


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

posture:
- most orderly and institutionally structured school progression
- soft academic-credential society
- exams matter, but not as absolute social destiny
- law, administration, education, rail, engineering, and agriculture routes are all formalized
- legal education becomes especially visible in higher branching


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

posture:
- discipline and service suitability are highly visible
- training routes and command routes are strong
- civilian education exists, but national-service timing affects life-course rhythm
- ages 25 to 28 compulsory service can interrupt or redirect later career branching


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

posture:
- formal progression exists but family, island culture, and craft continuity matter strongly
- arts, music, dance, ritual, maritime, fishery, and brewing routes are visible
- non-regular and family-business routes remain common


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

posture:
- doctrine and instability distort normal educational progression
- reconstruction labor and daily-hire work may pull students off standard routes
- interview and prior record can matter more than stable academic ladder in later placement


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

posture:
- city-by-city variation is strongest
- some cities resemble civic-institutional routes
- some resemble workshop, guild, or small-business routes
- progression is less standardized across the federation


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

School progression posture must remain strongly differentiated
so that later work and class outcomes feel nation-specific.
