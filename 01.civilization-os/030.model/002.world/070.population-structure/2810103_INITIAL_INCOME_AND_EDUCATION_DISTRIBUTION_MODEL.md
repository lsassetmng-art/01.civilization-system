# ============================================================
# 1. INCOME POSTURE
# ============================================================

helios_democratic_kingdom:
- strong worker and industrial middle
- upper technical and research tier present
- outer-belt lower income legacy remains

nova_commercial_federation:
- strongest high-income concentration
- strong finance and executive class
- strong low-to-mid service class
- high inequality posture

seiwa_state:
- thick middle class
- stable public-service and education-linked households
- lower inequality than Nova

gladia_military_alliance:
- command elite and defense-linked worker structure
- state-structured income posture
- lower luxury civilian tier

orpheus_oceanic_union:
- moderate income variation by island
- fisheries and brewing middle bands
- tourism-linked seasonal income variance

aurelia_federal_republic:
- collapse-distorted income structure
- doctrinal historical elite legacy
- broad instability

free_cities_union:
- city-by-city variance high
- merchant upper tier exists
- craft and service middle tier strong
- mixed urban precarity remains


# ============================================================
# 2. EDUCATION POSTURE
# ============================================================

helios_democratic_kingdom:
- strong technical education
- medium general education

nova_commercial_federation:
- strong elite and enterprise education
- high business and technical specialization

seiwa_state:
- strongest broad public education
- strongest child education and higher education spread

gladia_military_alliance:
- strong training and discipline education
- weaker civic-humanities breadth

orpheus_oceanic_union:
- moderate local schooling
- strong cultural and ritual transmission

aurelia_federal_republic:
- historically doctrinal education heavy
- current stable education degraded

free_cities_union:
- practical and city-specific education
- guild and civic learning strong
- weak unified national schooling

# 3. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Income and education distribution should explain
consumption, schooling, and political culture.
