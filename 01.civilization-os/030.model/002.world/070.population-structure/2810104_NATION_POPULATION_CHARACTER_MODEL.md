- dense urban commercial population
- executive high-income elite + service workforce split

- family-heavy, school-heavy, rail-linked stable society

gladia_military_alliance:
- young-adult heavy
- military and para-military population concentration
- command and security logic shapes households
- ages 25 to 28 are strongly structured by compulsory service for eligible citizens
- compulsory service is rear-support oriented during the service term, not front-line assignment

- island community population
- tolerant local variation
- sea-linked ritual and family structure

aurelia_federal_republic:

- historical believer and doctrinal legacy remains

free_cities_union:
- mixed urban civic population
- merchant, craft, and service population blend
- city-specific demographic variety
