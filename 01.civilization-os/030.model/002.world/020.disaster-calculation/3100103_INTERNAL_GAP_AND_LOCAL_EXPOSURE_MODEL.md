# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- outer districts show higher exposure to injury, weak recovery, and disorder spillover
- industrial and worker districts face stronger accident concentration than upper rings


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- dense commercial districts show high scam and pressure exposure
- premium districts remain better protected and recover faster
- port and nightlife districts carry distinct risk signatures


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- eastern frontier and industrial-port areas carry more exposure than the civic core
- differences exist but are moderated by stronger institutions


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- arsenal, training, and border zones carry higher accident and force-related exposure
- service households are buffered better than marginal detached households


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- island-by-island sea exposure is the dominant risk difference
- some islands are much more storm and ferry vulnerable than others


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- reconstruction zones and degraded districts carry severe local instability
- local protection depends on remaining state presence and political favor


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- port cities, workshop cities, and civic-rich cities differ sharply
- neighborhood-level guild strength strongly affects resilience


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Internal exposure gaps must remain visible
so that national averages do not erase lived danger.
