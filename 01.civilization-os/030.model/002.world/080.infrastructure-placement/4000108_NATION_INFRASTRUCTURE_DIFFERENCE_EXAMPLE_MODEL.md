# ============================================================
# 1. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- public corporations and orderly civic authority may dominate
- infrastructure legibility and standardized control may be strong


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- licensed private rail and premium transport operators may be common
- commercial station complexes may be strong, while authority remains transport-specific


# ============================================================
# 3. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- strategic and state-centered transport control may dominate
- major rail and port systems may lean toward strong centralized oversight


# ============================================================
# 4. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- smaller local authority patterns and regional port variation may be stronger
- infrastructure may favor community and island connectivity over heavy centralized grids


# ============================================================
# 5. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- infrastructure permissions may be rigid, uneven, or politically filtered
- access and build approval may be unstable across regions


# ============================================================
# 6. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city autonomy and port-specific governance may be strong
- one city may allow licensed operators while another remains public-heavy


# ============================================================
# 7. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Infrastructure governance
must vary by nation and city reality,
not merely by abstract type label.
