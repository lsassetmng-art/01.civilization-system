# ============================================================
# 2. OFFICIAL PROFILES
# ============================================================

## northern_continent
identity_keywords:
- engineering
- frontier
- expansion
- technical_workforce
- new_development

seed_bias:
- higher engineer population share
- stronger software / construction / manufacturing bias
- stronger infrastructure expansion bias
- stronger new-city and new-housing development bias

## central_continent
identity_keywords:
- civilization
- government
- economy
- finance
- technology
- child_education

seed_bias:
- strongest state and institutional density
- strongest banking / securities / listing density
- strongest school / academy / child education density
- highest city population density tendency

## southern_continent
identity_keywords:
- arts
- festival
- religion
- memory
- rebirth

seed_bias:
- stronger culture and arts concentration
- stronger ritual and religious concentration
- stronger exhibition / screening / tourism density
- stronger life-cycle ceremonial event density
