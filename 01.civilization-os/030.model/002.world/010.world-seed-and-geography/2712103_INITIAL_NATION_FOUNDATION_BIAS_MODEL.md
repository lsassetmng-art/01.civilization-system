# ============================================================
# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

Defines how continent-level Foundation meaning
biases the initial nation profile.


# ============================================================
# 2. NORTHERN CONTINENT NATION BIAS
# ============================================================

Recommended nation bias:
- engineer-heavy labor profile
- frontier growth posture
- new infrastructure expansion
- strong construction and software tendency
- strong manufacturing and logistics tendency
- higher housing development potential


# ============================================================
# 3. CENTRAL CONTINENT NATION BIAS
# ============================================================

Recommended nation bias:
- government and administrative density
- strong finance and market institutions
- strong technology and education institutions
- highest public service completeness
- highest major-city density
- strongest listing and business infrastructure


# ============================================================
# 4. SOUTHERN CONTINENT NATION BIAS
# ============================================================

Recommended nation bias:
- strong culture and arts identity
- strong ritual and religion presence
- strong tourism and festival density
- stronger memory and ceremonial life-event identity
- stronger exhibition and performance concentration


# ============================================================
# 5. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Continent meaning should bias nation seed data,
but does not force all nations on the same continent
to become identical.
