# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- noble-origin upper households strongly preserve family business continuity
- inheritance may override aptitude in upper strata
- status continuity is highly visible


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- business and asset succession are strongly financialized
- business continuity follows value, control, and management capacity
- premium households preserve wealth strategically


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- family continuity is orderly and legalistic
- family business and household assets are procedurally stabilized
- status continuity is less theatrical than in Helios


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- service honor, pension-like continuity, and household support are visible
- family status can be shaped by service record
- command lineage matters in limited elite paths


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- craft, boat, local shop, brewing, and fishery assets often pass through kinship lines
- succession is relational and island-embedded
- communal recognition matters


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- state and doctrine may intervene strongly
- succession stability depends on political legitimacy and surviving structure


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

succession_posture:
- workshop, guild, and small-business succession are strong
- city-specific legal and customary paths coexist
- local recognition can matter as much as formal process


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Business and status succession must reveal
how power survives across generations.
