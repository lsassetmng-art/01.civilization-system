# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- class-separated and prestige-sensitive facility placement should be visible
- elite-facing finance and commerce clusters may diverge from outer districts
- rougher and weaker civic information access may exist in poorer districts


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- dense commercial, finance, and mixed-use tower placement should be strongest
- premium direct stores, securities firms, and branded services should be highly visible
- graph and market boards should feel competitive and performance-heavy


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- practical separation of civic, finance, transport, education, and medical facilities should be strongest
- public information and transport-linked service hubs should be orderly
- mixed-use exists, but legibility remains high


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- disciplined civic and strategic facilities should dominate
- speculative or ornamental facility clustering should be weaker
- transport, support, and official-use facilities should be emphasized


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- smaller local hubs, communal markets, ritual facilities, and island service combinations should be visible
- large finance-centric towers are less central
- local trust modifies service ecology strongly


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- unstable, uneven, and politically filtered facility distribution must be expressible
- protected districts and neglected districts may diverge sharply
- information boards may be sparse, incomplete, or stressed


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city facility ecology must vary strongly
- port finance, guild commerce, local boards, and civic counters may all differ by city
- local regulation visibly affects placement patterns


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Facility placement and visibility
must explain the lived structure of each country,
not only service availability.
