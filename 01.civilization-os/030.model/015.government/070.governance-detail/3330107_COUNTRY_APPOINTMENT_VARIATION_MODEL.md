# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- old_noble_capture_high
- ruler_favor_logic_nontrivial
- upper_offices_can_be_formally_institutional_but_practically_patronage_based
- family_and_lineage_weight_high

pipeline_note:
- lower_and_middle_career_paths_may_use_aptitude_or_service_filters
- upper_paths_remain_more_capture_prone

removal_note:
- protection_by_patronage_high
- informal_survival_strength_high


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- business_and_technical_elite_weight_high
- high_performance_story_visible
- real_selection_filtered_by_capital_and_network_access

pipeline_note:
- commercial_success_and strategic utility matter strongly
- asic-linked elite pathways must be supported

removal_note:
- performance_failure_or scandal risk high
- replacement speed higher than in Seiwa


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- strongest procedural and qualification-based legitimacy
- bureaucracy and examination logic highly visible

pipeline_note:
- ministry_career_pipeline strong
- merit and process visibility high

removal_note:
- formal review and controlled dismissal stronger
- arbitrary purge pressure lower than in Helios or Aurelia


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- service_record_and_loyalty_high
- command_suitability_high
- military_and_security_sensitive_roles strongly filtered

pipeline_note:
- command and service pathway dominant in top security posts

removal_note:
- emergency and command removal possible
- survival depends heavily on loyalty and operational trust


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- local legitimacy and relationship credibility high
- island trust and communal standing matter strongly

pipeline_note:
- less formalized elite ladders
- relational succession and local continuity visible

removal_note:
- hard purge structure weaker
- informal replacement and consensus adjustment more common


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- doctrine_and loyalty_can_override competence strongly
- protected insider structure must be supported

pipeline_note:
- formal merit pipeline weak
- survival and ideological alignment matter heavily

removal_note:
- purge risk high
- protected circles and exposed circles differ sharply


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

appointment_note:
- city ecology determines elite selection style
- guild, city, merchant, and civic paths may all coexist

pipeline_note:
- no single unified elite ladder
- local legitimacy and negotiated elevation matter strongly

removal_note:
- city-by-city difference high
- negotiated exit more common than hard centralized purge


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Appointment Builder must support
sharp divergence between
formal eligibility,
real access,
and survival capacity.
