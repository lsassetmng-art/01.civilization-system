# ============================================================

- Overview: nation snapshot and build status
- Identity: nation name, type, capital, canonical identity
- Geography: terrain, islands, access, regional form
- Population: scale, distribution, class and demographic base
- Economy: industry, trade, transport, cost burden, signature sectors
- Society and Culture: family, religion, communication, leisure, appearance, time, mobility, consumption
- Zoning and Infrastructure: city form, ports, rail, districts, institutions
- Military and Security: armed force, policing, conscription, support posture
- Power Structure: structure-layer routing and scope-layer routing
- Legislature: chamber and legislative control
- Executive: ruler, government, ministries, emergency chain
- Appointment and Elites: office pipelines and elite access
- Factions and Influence: pressure groups, lobby, corruption, patronage
- Lock and Validation: structure lock state and errors
- Preview and Summary: quick reference and matrix view
- Persistence and Export: save, snapshot, publish-ready state
