# 1. CORE SPATIAL RULE
# ============================================================

Helios preserves visible spatial traces
of its former kingdom-era class hierarchy
even after revolution weakened the monarchy.

The same historical class layering also remains visible
in education structure and facility placement.

- elite residential district
- upper institutional housing
- senior researcher and upper management housing
- AI industrial school legacy


# ============================================================
# 4. SECOND OUTER RING

- neighborhood commerce
- mixed citizen housing
- daily-life urban core

# ============================================================

- former poor districts
- former slave residential areas
- worker housing
- industrial support housing
- redevelopment zones
- low-income recovery zones

Helios urban structure preserves a layered memory
of kingdom hierarchy beneath its current industrial state form.
