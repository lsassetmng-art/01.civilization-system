# ============================================================
# 1. CORE SPATIAL RULE
# ============================================================

Nova is a market-state that grew from a merchant union.

Its spatial structure prioritizes commerce,
corporate concentration, and alliance-financed security
over fortress-style territorial defense.


# ============================================================
# 2. CAPITAL CORE
# ============================================================

federal_market_core_zone:
- civilization-wide central market
- federal conference buildings
- commercial governance buildings
- trade plazas
- market coordination facilities


# ============================================================
# 3. EAST OF CAPITAL
# ============================================================

asic_corporate_core_zone:
- ASIC headquarters tower cluster
- ultra-high corporate skyline
- AI computing and enterprise command core
- practical power center of the nation


# ============================================================
# 4. NORTH OF ASIC
# ============================================================

international_top_firm_tower_zone:
- top foreign company towers
- international business offices
- elite enterprise district


# ============================================================
# 5. SOUTH OF ASIC
# ============================================================

executive_luxury_residential_zone:
- high-end residential district
- executive housing
- upper-tier service facilities
- premium urban living zone


# ============================================================
# 6. NORTHEAST COASTAL BELT
# ============================================================

northeast_coastal_industrial_mountain_belt:
- mountain-adjacent industrial districts
- coastal heavy industry
- logistics and production support


# ============================================================
# 7. SOUTHERN COMMERCIAL BELT
# ============================================================

southern_commercial_expansion_belt:
- large-scale commercial expansion
- retail, finance, service concentration
- mixed commercial urban growth


# ============================================================
# 8. SECURITY NOTE
# ============================================================

Nova historically relied on alliance and financing
to secure protection, especially through Gladia-backed deterrence,
rather than fortress geography.


# ============================================================
# 9. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Nova is a commercial-market federation
whose state geography is organized around market power and corporate density.
