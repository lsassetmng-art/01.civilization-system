# ============================================================
# 1. CORE SPATIAL RULE
# ============================================================

Free Cities Union has weak federation-wide zoning uniformity.

Each city holds its own local land-use rules,
while the union shares only minimal common standards
for safety, movement, and trade.


# ============================================================
# 2. LAND-USE CHARACTER
# ============================================================

city_specific_autonomous_zoning:
- strong local variation
- weak nationwide zoning unification
- historic and negotiated urban form

mixed_use_heavy_posture:
- residential zones often mix with commerce
- commerce often mixes with workshop industry
- port, market, housing, and craft belts are often adjacent


# ============================================================
# 3. COMMON UNION-LAYER RULES
# ============================================================

shared_minimum_rules:
- movement access
- trade route continuity
- minimum safety standards
- conflict mediation baseline


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Free Cities spatial order is diverse, negotiated,
and mixed-use heavy rather than centrally standardized.
