# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- formal tax burden not extreme
- effective burden becomes harsher because weak households receive little public buffering
- private means and inheritance sharply change actual living pressure


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- household cost is shaped more by market structure than by simple tax level
- service quality, district, and income tier create extreme lived cost differences


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- tax and public support balance is orderly
- households bear moderate formal burden in exchange for predictable institutional support


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- state support is selective and service-linked
- households connected to service structure are more protected than detached households


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- lower formal tax and market burden in many areas
- family and island support substitute for expensive institutional systems


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- formal systems are unstable
- effective burden depends on income disruption, day labor continuity, and remaining state channels


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

style_summary:
- burden logic is fragmented by city
- taxes, services, and household survival vary with local rules and civic capacity


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Tax and public-support style must explain
why equal income does not produce equal lived stability across nations.
