# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- upper households treat marriage more as lineage and continuity management
- lower households experience marriage as economic stabilization pressure
- the same institution carries different meanings by class


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- elite households treat partnership as status and strategic compatibility
- ordinary households experience romance under rent, childcare, and career pressure
- autonomy exists, but cost filters it sharply


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- family ideals are shared relatively broadly
- institutional support reduces the gap between moral expectation and practical possibility


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- service families internalize duty-heavy marriage more strongly
- detached civilians may feel family pressure without equal support


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- island differences matter more than abstract class difference alone
- stronger kinship islands produce more collective child-raising norms


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- protected insiders and unstable outsiders experience family duty very differently
- doctrine is strongest where supervision is strongest


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

internal_gap_notes:
- city ecology matters more than federation identity
- guild, workshop, port, and civic-rich cities produce different family expectations


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Internal family-culture gaps must remain visible
so that nation averages do not flatten lived family meaning.
