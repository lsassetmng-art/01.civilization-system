# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- strongest inherited upper ownership continuity
- old noble families preserve business and asset concentration
- ownership and status transfer often override open competition


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- ownership is concentrated through commercial success, finance, and scaling capital
- inherited wealth exists, but active performance and capital control remain central


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- household property continuity exists, but concentrated dynastic control is softer
- institutional order reduces chaotic transfer risk


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- service and command pathways influence stable ownership access
- extreme dynastic concentration is weaker than Helios


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- family boats, local enterprises, craft assets, and household property matter most
- relational succession is more important than large formal capital blocks


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- unstable continuity and state interference weaken secure ownership transfer
- remaining protected assets may depend on political positioning


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

ownership_posture:
- workshop, guild, and merchant ownership are important
- concentration varies strongly by city and local rules

# 8. FINAL RULE

why class continuity reproduces differently across nations.
