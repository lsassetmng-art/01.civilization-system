# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- class and district still shape branch quality
- technical and AI-industrial prestige routes are strongest
- upper households convert elite tracks into ownership and status more easily


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- business, economics, commerce, and enterprise routes are dominant
- practical skill and earning potential outweigh formal prestige more than in Seiwa
- ASIC route remains highly technical and performance-heavy


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- most stable and legible academic progression
- law and public-administration routes are especially visible
- broad credentialing exists, but society remains only softly academic-credentialist


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- training and service suitability remain strong filters
- national service overlays later educational and occupational timing


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- arts, ritual, music, dance, maritime, and craft routes are socially legible
- family and island network routes remain strong alternatives to formal progression


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- progression is less stable and more easily disrupted
- reconstruction and ideological filtering distort normal school-to-career flow


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

difference_summary:
- branch logic varies by city, district, guild, and workshop culture
- no single federation-wide prestige ladder fully dominates


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Branching difference must visibly affect later labor,
status, and household formation.
