# 2. NATION NOTES
# ============================================================

helios:
- industrial safety, work allocation, and production honor events are strong
- old family-business succession remains visible in upper firms

- profit, sales, investment, hiring, and showcase events are strongest
- company events are closely tied to money-making performance
- practical skill and earning results are publicly valued

seiwa:
- public-trust, procedural order, and employee stability events are strong
- rail and public infrastructure employers carry formal annual rhythm

gladia:
- service honor, training completion, and readiness events are strong
- conscription-related employer adjustments can affect company cycles

orpheus:
- small-business, family-business, craft, and seasonal work events are common
- formal corporate rhythm is weaker than community and trade rhythm

aurelia:
- reconstruction labor and state-directed work events dominate where stable
- day-labor and project completion cycles matter

free_cities_union:
- small firm, guild, workshop, and market-season events dominate
- hiring logic remains local and practical
