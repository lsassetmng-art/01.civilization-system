# ============================================================
# 1. HELIOS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- class differences in care access and continuity must remain expressible
- upper-class care and outer-district care may diverge strongly
- weak support systems should make discharge continuity harsher for fragile households


# ============================================================
# 2. NOVA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- premium care, speed, branded trust, and class-based care separation should be supportable
- ordinary urban care pressure and premium medical channels may diverge sharply
- efficiency and service-value visibility should be strong


# ============================================================
# 3. SEIWA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- stable, orderly, and institutionally legible care flow should be strongest
- community clinics, referral order, and predictable discharge continuity should be emphasized
- hospitals should feel integrated into everyday civic life


# ============================================================
# 4. GLADIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- recovery, rehabilitation, service injury handling, and reintegration support should be strongly visible
- military-linked care pathways may overlap with civil care more than elsewhere
- structured continuity should be emphasized


# ============================================================
# 5. ORPHEUS
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- island clinics, local healer trust, ferry-linked referral, and family support continuity must be strongly expressible
- distance and weather should materially affect care access
- communal care logic should remain visible


# ============================================================
# 6. AURELIA
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- unstable care access, fragile supply, and protection gaps must be strongly expressible
- insiders and outsiders may experience very different medical continuity
- crisis and overflow should feel harsher and more frequent


# ============================================================
# 7. FREE CITIES UNION
# ============================================================

variation_note:
- city-by-city medical ecology must be supported
- civic hospitals, merchant-funded hospitals, local clinics, and port emergency systems may diverge heavily
- local governance should often shape care more than federation identity


# ============================================================
# 8. FINAL RULE
# ============================================================

Medical UI must visibly change by country and local care ecology,
not only by facility size.
