## 1. Purpose

Defines the minimum document bundle needed to restore the current posture safely.

## 2. Minimum Restoration Bundle

The minimum restoration bundle is:

- current posture one-page
- canonical posture handoff
- canonical baseline snapshot
- final decision pack integrated status
- operator entrypoint master

## 3. Extended Restoration Bundle

When confusion persists, extend with:

- steady-state and change-initiation integrated status
- cross-session continuity and onboarding integrated status

## 4. Bundle Rule

Use the minimum bundle first.
Only extend when the minimum bundle does not resolve confusion.

## 5. Hard Rule

Do not jump straight to old detailed packs during restoration unless traceability investigation is explicitly required.
