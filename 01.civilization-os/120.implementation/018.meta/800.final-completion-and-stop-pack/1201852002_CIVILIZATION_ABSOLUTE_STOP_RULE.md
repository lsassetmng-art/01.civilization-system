## 1. Purpose

Defines the absolute stop posture after final completion certification.

## 2. Absolute Stop Rule

After final completion:

- no ordinary design-expansion continuation
- no casual new control-pack creation
- no hidden reopening of settled request portfolio
- no semantic movement outside governed reentry

## 3. Allowed Post-Completion Movement

Allowed movement only through:

- final reentry gate
- recovery-critical correction
- explicit governed owner-side redecision
- explicit integrity correction that does not bypass governance

## 4. Hard Rule

Completion means stop by default.
