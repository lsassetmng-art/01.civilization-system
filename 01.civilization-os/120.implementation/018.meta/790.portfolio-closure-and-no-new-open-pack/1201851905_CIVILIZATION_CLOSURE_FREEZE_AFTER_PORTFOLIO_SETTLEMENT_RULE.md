## 1. Purpose

Defines the freeze posture after the portfolio reaches settlement.

## 2. Freeze Rule

After portfolio settlement:

- no routine new request opening
- no silent queue reactivation
- no hidden semantic continuation
- no casual reclassification of stopped or closed cases

## 3. Allowed Post-Settlement Movement

Allowed movement only through:

- recovery-critical detection
- explicit governed redecision
- explicit closure-integrity correction
- final completion certification sequence

## 4. Hard Rule

Settlement freeze is stronger than ordinary steady-state queue handling.

## 5. Acceptance Checklist

- freeze rule fixed
- allowed post-settlement movement fixed
- settlement-freeze-stronger-than-normal fixed
