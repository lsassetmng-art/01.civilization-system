## 1. Purpose

Defines what may be treated as archival reference
and what must remain active canonical surface.

## 2. Active Canonical Surface

The following must remain active first-line reference:

- canonical posture handoff
- final decision pack
- decision reconsideration governance
- baseline freeze and successor pack
- handoff / future-use pack

## 3. Archival Reference Surface

The following may be treated as deeper archival reference when not needed first:

- detailed intermediate exact-design packs
- older partial execution packs
- deeper subordinate planning packs already summarized upstream

## 4. Archive Boundary Rule

Archive treatment means:
- lower reading priority
- preserved for traceability
- not deleted
- not used as first operational entry point

## 5. Hard Rule

Archival reference does not mean obsolete or invalid.
It means non-primary for current posture handling.

## 6. Acceptance Checklist

- active canonical surface fixed
- archival reference surface fixed
- archive boundary meaning fixed
