## 1. Purpose

Defines the high-level supersession map across the late-stage canonical packs.

## 2. Supersession Principle

Late-stage packs are layered, not randomly replaceable.

Interpret them in the following order:

1. final decision pack
2. decision revision and reconsideration governance pack
3. handoff and future-use pack
4. baseline freeze and successor pack
5. archive / maintenance / supersession pack

## 3. Interpretation Rule

Later packs do not erase earlier canonical content.
They define how earlier canonical content should be:
- read
- preserved
- revised
- audited
- archived

## 4. Hard Rule

Do not treat archive or maintenance documents as direct decision overrides.
