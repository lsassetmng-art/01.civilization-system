## Exact Public Eligibility Boundary

Public office eligibility must remain separate from:

- civil service eligibility
- military mobilization eligibility
- current office holding status
- hiring or employment status

## Exact Eligibility Surface

Eligibility implementation must explicitly define:

- qualification source
- restriction source
- review result
- validity posture
- downstream usage binding

Eligibility may gate office access,
but it is not identical to office activation truth.
