## Exact Placement Non-Ownership Rule

Persona placement in CivilizationOS is a world-placement fact.

It does not mean CivilizationOS owns internal mutable persona canon.

Placement may define:
- where a persona is placed in the world
- what world-side participation is enabled
- what world-side restrictions apply

Placement must not become a surrogate for PersonaOS mutable-state storage.
