## Exact Territory And Zoning Split

Territory answers where a unit belongs.
Zoning answers what a unit may be used for.
These must not be represented as the same canonical field.

## Exact Territory Zoning Surface

The following must remain separate:

- nation territory
- city territory
- district
- zone definition
- zoning preset
- zoning assignment
- zoning compatibility result
- map projection

Map view is projection only and not canonical territory truth.
