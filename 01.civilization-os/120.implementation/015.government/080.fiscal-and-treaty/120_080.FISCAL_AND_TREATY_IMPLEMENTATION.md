## Exact Treaty And Fiscal Boundary

Treaty lifecycle truth and fiscal execution truth must remain separate.

Government-side treaty implementation owns:

- treaty draft
- negotiation
- ratification
- activation
- suspension
- termination
- supersede lineage

Government-side fiscal implementation may own:

- fiscal rule bundle
- budget release gate
- reviewed fiscal activation boundary

But transaction-level financial truth remains outside treaty lifecycle truth.

## Exact Treaty Activation Rule

A treaty may not become active unless:

- ratification is complete
- activation is explicit
- reviewed authority is satisfied
- predecessor/successor conflict is resolved where applicable
