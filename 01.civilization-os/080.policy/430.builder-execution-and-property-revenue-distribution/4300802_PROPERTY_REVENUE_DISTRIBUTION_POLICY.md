# POLICY

Builder-related and property-related payments
must be internally distributed to proper recipients.

Required recipient categories include:

- construction company
- land owner
- nation-side fee or tax receiver
- real-estate mediation layer where applicable


# FINAL RULE

Undifferentiated property payment treatment
is prohibited in internal accounting.
