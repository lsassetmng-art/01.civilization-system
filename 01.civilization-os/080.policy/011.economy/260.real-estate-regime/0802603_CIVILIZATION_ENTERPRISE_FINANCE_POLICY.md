Define policy for enterprise finance

# ============================================================
# 1. CORE POLICY
# ============================================================

Enterprise finance must remain:
- internal to Civilization
- company-aware
- nation-governed
- DB-controlled
- explainable
- fail-closed on unresolved rule chains


# ============================================================
# 2. PRODUCT POLICY
# ============================================================

Nation DB rules may control:
- which products exist
- who may borrow
- which industries qualify
- which guarantees qualify
- which collateral qualifies
- which sectors need approval


# ============================================================
# 3. ERP NON-LINK POLICY
# ============================================================

The following must not depend on ERP:
- Civilization enterprise loan application
- Civilization enterprise loan screening
- Civilization enterprise loan contract
- Civilization enterprise repayment
- Civilization enterprise default and recovery

ERP is out of scope.


# ============================================================
# 4. FINAL POLICY RULE
# ============================================================

Civilization enterprise finance policy must be:
- internal
- nation-controlled
- DB-controlled
- ERP-independent
