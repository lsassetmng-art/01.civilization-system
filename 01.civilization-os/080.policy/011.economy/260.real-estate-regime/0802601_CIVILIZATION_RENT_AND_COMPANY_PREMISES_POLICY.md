Define policy for company premises and rent inside CivilizationOS.


# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PRINCIPLE
# ============================================================

Civilization company premises and rent must remain
fully inside Civilization legal/economic boundaries.


# ============================================================
# 2. COMPANY PREMISES POLICY
# ============================================================

A Civilization company may have:
- registered premises
- operating premises
- owned premises
- leased premises
- allocated or granted premises

All such relations must be backed by valid property rights
or valid occupancy basis.


# ============================================================
# 3. RENT POLICY
# ============================================================

Rent must be:
- contract-based
- nation-currency-based
- collection-rule-based
- default-aware

Rent must not be treated as ERP-facing data.


# ============================================================
# 4. ERP NON-LINK POLICY
# ============================================================

The following must not be sent to ERP:
- Civilization rent contracts
- Civilization rent collection records
- Civilization company premises records
- Civilization property ownership/lease records

ERP is for real-world companies only.


# ============================================================
# 5. FINAL POLICY RULE
# ============================================================

Civilization company premises and rent are internal to Civilization.

Core summary:

- valid premises require valid right or occupancy basis
- rent is collected in nation currency
- no ERP linkage is allowed
