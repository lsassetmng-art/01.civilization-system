# POLICY

In Nova,
ASIC is the owner of:

- multi-tenant corporate buildings containing multiple world companies
- storefront properties in ASIC City
- employee dormitory properties


# ROLE BOUNDARY

Tenant,
operator,
occupant,
leaseholder,
and assigned resident
must remain distinct from owner.


# FINAL RULE

Nova covered property categories
must resolve owner-side income to ASIC
unless an explicit override contract exists.
