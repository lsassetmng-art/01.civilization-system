# ============================================================
# 1. CORE POLICY
# ============================================================

Extended nation profile editing must remain part of nation draft state
until publication and activation.

This is mandatory.


# ============================================================
# 2. CONSISTENCY POLICY
# ============================================================

Nation symbolic, governance, social, economic, cultural,
service, and event profiles must remain internally consistent
and validation-visible.

Contradictions must not be silently ignored.


# ============================================================
# 3. FINAL POLICY RULE
# ============================================================

Extended nation builder policy must be:
- profile-aware
- consistency-aware
- validation-aware
- fail-closed
