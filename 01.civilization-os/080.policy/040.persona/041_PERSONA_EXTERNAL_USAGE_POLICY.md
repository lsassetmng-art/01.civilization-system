## PURPOSE
Define policy for external commercial use of Persona-derived artifacts.

## RULES
- Persona original state remains owned by PersonaOS
- only releasable external units may be sold or exposed
- CivilizationOS may commercialize usage but may not override Persona rights
- invalid, revoked, or expired Persona access must disable use
- external use must respect PersonaOS license and access grant state
