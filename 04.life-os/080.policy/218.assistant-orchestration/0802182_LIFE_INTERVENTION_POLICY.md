purpose:
Defines the policy boundaries for interventions, reminders, and support prompts.

policy:
- interventions must be driven by LifeOS facts or signals
- interventions must respect silence windows
- interventions must minimize interruption burden
- sensitive content must be minimized in visible surfaces
- repeated ignored prompts should reduce aggressive repetition
- urgent alerts may bypass ordinary ranking only under explicit alert rules
