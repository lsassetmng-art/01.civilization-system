- personal-finance-lite multi-currency handling

- business-side bookkeeping

- LifeOS remains a private-life support OS
- language and locale are separate concepts
- source currency and display currency are separate concepts
- multilingual output must remain structurally stable
- multi-currency output must remain understandable
