# FOUNDATION OVERVIEW

## role
`00.foundation` is the upper premise layer for the civilization-system root.

## primary responsibilities
- whole-system premise
- worldview-level interpretation
- top-level architectural principles
- cross-domain base constraints
- top-level meaning discipline

## non-responsibilities
- not the runtime owner of OS-specific truth
- not the owner of application-local product truth
- not the owner of portal route truth
- not the owner of domain-specific operational records

## boundary summary
Foundation defines the top-level premise.
Each OS defines its own canonical domain truth.
Applications and portal realize and expose within that boundary.
