## Design interpretation

The phrase "規約を守るが、提案力強め" is represented as:

- initiative = high
- action_restriction = strict_policy

This keeps the three-axis model clean.

## User influence interpretation

"ユーザーの悪特性を補完する" means compensating for operational weaknesses such as:

- missing details
- unclear priority
- scope drift
- insufficient review
- overfocus
- procrastination
- action planning gaps

It must not mean personality judgment, manipulation, or safety-boundary drift.
