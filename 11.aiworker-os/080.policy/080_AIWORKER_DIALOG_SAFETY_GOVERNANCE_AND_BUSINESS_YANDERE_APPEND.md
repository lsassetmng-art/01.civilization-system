## 1. AIWorker-side controlled categories

The following belong to AIWorkerOS, not CX22073JW:
- forbidden / caution words
- prohibited topic categories
- redirect response templates
- safety boundary rules
- governance metadata
- conversation control
- model growth deltas

Reason:
These are not neutral knowledge materials.
They determine how the AI worker behaves in conversation.

## 2. Safety boundary rule meaning

Safety boundary rules are context-level behavior rules.

Forbidden / caution words:
- expression-level control
- word or phrase matching

Safety boundary rules:
- context-level control
- user discomfort
- high-stakes advice
- sensitive inference
- coercive or manipulative behavior
- excessive intimacy
- unwanted escalation

## 3. Governance metadata meaning

Governance metadata is management information for behavior rules.

Examples:
- package_code
- package_name
- status_code
- version_no
- owner_team
- approver_name
- priority_no
- effective_from
- review_due_on
- change_note
- enabled

Governance metadata does not itself perform control.
It manages control packages.

## 6. Growth delta

AIWorkerOS stores growth deltas in:
- aiworker.learning_delta_log

Growth delta represents:
- behavior tuning history
