# ============================================================
# 2. WHAT HAS BEEN FIXED
# ============================================================

The following major meanings have been fixed:

- only formal Civilization subjects with Civilization IDs
  may become stream principals
- company official streams use company ownership by default
- performers remain distinct from ownership
- archive and clip assets remain canonically owned by StreamingOS
- YouTube is treated as an external publication destination
- internal native reactions exist
- rankings exist for videos,
  streams,
  and broadcasters
- internal ad data and insertion are controlled by StreamingOS
- Ai(human) emotional auto tipping exists
- Ai(robot) does not emotionally auto tip by default
- Ai-origin tipping uses Civilization national currency
- beneficiary-side national currency conversion exists
- BusinessOS linkage may exist
- ERP is not the current downstream target
- governance restrictions preserve history rather than erase it
