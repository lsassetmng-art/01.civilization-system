# 1. PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This document gives the root overview
of StreamingOS.

StreamingOS is the canonical streaming platform
inside Civilization
for creator activity,
viewer continuity,
moderation,
notification,
and company-side oversight.

# ============================================================
# 2. PLATFORM SCOPE
# ============================================================

StreamingOS includes:
- creator-side streaming and publication
- viewer-side discovery and watch continuity
- tipping and monetization interpretation
- moderation and restoration
- notification and alert handling
- company / affiliation oversight

StreamingOS is not limited to
simple stream delivery only.

- tablet

- creator surfaces
