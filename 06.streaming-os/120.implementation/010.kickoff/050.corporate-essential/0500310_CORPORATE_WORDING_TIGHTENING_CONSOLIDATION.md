purpose:
Consolidates wording-tightening points for K5 corporate-essential.

tightening_points:

public_vs_oversight_visibility:
- public channel visibility means viewer-safe channel presentation only
- oversight visibility means company-scoped oversight-safe projection only
- oversight visibility must never be treated as public channel detail expansion

affiliation_vs_ownership:
- affiliation expresses relationship/visibility semantics only
- affiliation must never widen into ownership mutation authority
- ownership mutation remains limited to owner or granted company authority paths

channel_identity_boundary:
- channel identity remains distinct from principal identity
- channel profile state remains distinct from oversight state
- affiliated streamer reference remains distinct from both channel identity and ownership identity

dashboard_wording:
- corporate dashboards are company-scoped operational/oversight views
- dashboards are not public channel profile substitutes
- company summaries may reference public-facing counts without becoming public-facing objects

support_path_wording:
- support-path access remains exceptional and audit-linked
- support-path access must not silently widen public or oversight-safe visibility
