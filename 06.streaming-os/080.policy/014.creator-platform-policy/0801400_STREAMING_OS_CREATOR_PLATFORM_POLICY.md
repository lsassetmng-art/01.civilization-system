# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines creator-side platform rules
inside StreamingOS.

# ============================================================
# 2. CREATOR SELF-MANAGEMENT POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide creator-facing self-management surfaces
for:
- owned videos
- owned streams
- owned archives
- owned clips
- creator-visible revenue summaries
- creator-visible ranking summaries
- creator settings

These are self-facing control surfaces,
not public-facing channel surfaces.

# ============================================================
# 3. UPLOAD / STUDIO POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide native upload and studio preparation
for:
- upload intake
- draft save
- metadata setting
- thumbnail assignment
- visibility setting
- schedule setting
- review visibility
- publish request execution

# ============================================================
# 4. EDITING BOUNDARY POLICY
# ============================================================

Video editing may be provided by a separate application.

StreamingOS is not required to own
the full standalone video-editing function.

StreamingOS must own upload and publish preparation.

# ============================================================
# 5. DRAFT PRESERVATION POLICY
# ============================================================

Studio drafts should remain preservable,
resumable,
and cancellable.

Draft existence must remain explicit.

# ============================================================
# 6. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide creator-facing self-management
and upload/studio preparation as native platform functions,
while preserving a boundary between
publish preparation
and standalone editing applications.
