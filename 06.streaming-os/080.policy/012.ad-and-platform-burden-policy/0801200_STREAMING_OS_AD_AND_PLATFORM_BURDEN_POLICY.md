# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines internal ad handling posture
and burden limits for viewers.

# ============================================================
# 2. INTERNAL AD OWNERSHIP POLICY
# ============================================================

Advertisement data,
insertion rules,
and display history
shall be held inside StreamingOS.

Internal ad control shall remain canonical inside StreamingOS.

# ============================================================
# 3. BURDEN POSTURE POLICY
# ============================================================

Ad burden shall use YouTube-equivalent burden posture
as the baseline reference.

This means:
- no intentionally excessive insertion
- viewing experience remains protected
- insertion burden should remain explainable

# ============================================================
# 4. INTERNAL / EXTERNAL DISTINCTION POLICY
# ============================================================

Internal StreamingOS ads
shall remain distinct from
external platform advertising behavior.

External publish platforms
do not replace internal ad policy.

# ============================================================
# 5. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall govern internal ads internally
and shall use a YouTube-equivalent burden posture baseline
without collapsing internal ad behavior
into external platform advertising behavior.
