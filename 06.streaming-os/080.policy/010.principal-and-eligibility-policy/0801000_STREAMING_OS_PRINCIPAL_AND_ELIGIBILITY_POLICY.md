# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines who may become a stream principal
and under what conditions.

# ============================================================
# 2. ELIGIBLE PRINCIPALS
# ============================================================

Eligible principal types:
- individual
- group
- company
- ai_human

These must be formal Civilization subjects
with valid Civilization IDs.

# ============================================================
# 3. INELIGIBLE PRINCIPALS
# ============================================================

Ineligible principal types:
- persona
- ai_robot
- anonymous subject
- external unregistered subject
- any subject without valid Civilization ID

# ============================================================
# 4. ACTIVE STATUS REQUIREMENT
# ============================================================

A subject must not only possess Civilization ID,
but must also be operationally eligible,
including:
- active subject status
- no stream suspension
- no blocking governance restriction
- required permission satisfaction

# ============================================================
# 5. CORPORATE OWNERSHIP POLICY
# ============================================================

For official corporate streams:
- principal shall be the company
- primary rights holder shall default to the company
- primary revenue beneficiary shall default to the company

Employee or affiliated participation
shall not automatically override ownership.

# ============================================================
# 6. PERFORMER DISTINCTION POLICY
# ============================================================

Performer participation is participation only by default.

Performer participation does not automatically alter:
- stream principal
- primary rights holder
- primary revenue beneficiary

# ============================================================
# 7. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

Only formal Civilization subjects
with valid Civilization IDs
may become stream principals.

Corporate official streams
shall use the company as principal by default.

Performer participation
shall remain distinct from ownership.
