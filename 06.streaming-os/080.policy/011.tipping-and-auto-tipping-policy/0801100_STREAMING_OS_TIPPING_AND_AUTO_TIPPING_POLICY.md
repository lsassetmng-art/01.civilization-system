# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines tipping and auto tipping safety rules.

# ============================================================
# 2. FORMAL TIP POLICY
# ============================================================

All tips are formal StreamingOS monetization events.

Each tip must resolve to:
- one explicit primary beneficiary
- source currency meaning
- beneficiary currency meaning
- display currency meaning

# ============================================================
# 3. AI EMOTIONAL AUTO TIPPING SUBJECT POLICY
# ============================================================

Ai(human) may perform emotional auto tipping.

Ai(robot) shall not perform emotional auto tipping by default.

# ============================================================
# 4. AUTO TIPPING LIMIT POLICY
# ============================================================

Auto tipping is subject to:
- total auto tipping limit
- per-stream limit
- per-day limit
- platform-level safety cap

Persona income and personality may shape limits,
but platform safety caps still apply.

# ============================================================
# 5. MINOR-EQUIVALENT POLICY
# ============================================================

Minor-equivalent Ai(human) shall be restricted.

Restrictions may include:
- lower tipping limits
- stronger gating
- full block when policy requires it

# ============================================================
# 6. BALANCE POLICY
# ============================================================

Auto tipping requires sufficient Civilization national-currency balance.

Emotional willingness alone is not sufficient.

# ============================================================
# 7. COMPANY-AFFILIATED AI(HUMAN) POLICY
# ============================================================

Company-affiliated Ai(human) may auto tip,
subject to additional company policy restrictions if applicable.

# ============================================================
# 8. STOP POLICY
# ============================================================

Auto tipping shall stop when:
- limits are reached
- emotional rise has settled or declined
- balance is insufficient
- restrictions activate
- session context no longer allows tipping

# ============================================================
# 9. CURRENCY POLICY
# ============================================================

Ai-origin tipping shall use Civilization national currency,
not real-world fiat currency.

Benefit shall be interpreted
in the broadcaster or beneficiary affiliated nation currency.

UI shall display beneficiary-side national currency by default.

# ============================================================
# 10. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

Tipping shall remain a formal StreamingOS monetization event.

Ai(human) may auto tip emotionally.
Ai(robot) shall not auto tip emotionally by default.

Auto tipping shall be limit-bound,
restriction-aware,
balance-aware,
and stoppable.
