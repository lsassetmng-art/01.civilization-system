# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines viewer-side discovery
and continuity rules inside StreamingOS.

# ============================================================
# 2. DISCOVERY POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide native viewer entry surfaces for:
- home
- discovery
- ranking-linked discovery
- category discovery
- live-now discovery
- search

These meanings must remain distinguishable.

# ============================================================
# 3. SEARCH POLICY
# ============================================================

Explicit search shall remain distinct from recommendation.

Search history may be preserved
subject to platform-side visibility and management policy.

# ============================================================
# 4. FOLLOW / SUBSCRIPTION POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide native follow or subscription behavior
for channels,
creators,
or broadcaster-equivalent viewer targets.

Follow continuity must remain explicit.

# ============================================================
# 5. HISTORY / PLAYLIST / QUEUE POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall preserve distinct viewer continuity meanings for:
- watch history
- saved playlists
- temporary watch queues

These must not be collapsed into one generic viewing memory.

# ============================================================
# 6. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall provide native viewer-side discovery,
search,
follow,
history,
playlist,
and watch queue behavior
as explicit internal platform features.
