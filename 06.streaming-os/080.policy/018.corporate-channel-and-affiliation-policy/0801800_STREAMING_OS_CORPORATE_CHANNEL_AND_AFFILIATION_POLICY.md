# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines company-side channel
and affiliation rules inside StreamingOS.

# ============================================================
# 2. OFFICIAL CHANNEL POLICY
# ============================================================

Official company channels
shall preserve company ownership meaning explicitly.

Official company streaming should not be visually or semantically
collapsed into individual participation.

# ============================================================
# 3. AFFILIATED STREAMER POLICY
# ============================================================

Affiliated streamer participation
may remain visible and meaningful,
but does not by itself replace company ownership.

Affiliation meaning and ownership meaning
must remain distinct.

# ============================================================
# 4. COMPANY OVERSIGHT POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS may provide company-side visibility
for official channels,
affiliated streamers,
and company-owned stream follow-up.

# ============================================================
# 5. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall preserve explicit policy distinctions between:
- official company ownership
- affiliated streamer participation
- company oversight visibility
