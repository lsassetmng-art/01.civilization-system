# ============================================================
# 1. POLICY PURPOSE
# ============================================================

This policy defines native reaction and ranking policy.

# ============================================================
# 2. NATIVE REACTION POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall support native reactions including:
- like
- dislike
- favorite
- comment

These reactions are internal platform features,
not external platform shadows.

# ============================================================
# 3. DISLIKE POLICY
# ============================================================

Dislike is a valid native reaction signal.

It is not by itself a governance punishment.

# ============================================================
# 4. FAVORITE POLICY
# ============================================================

Favorite represents stronger or longer affinity meaning
than a single like event.

Favorite shall remain distinct from like.

# ============================================================
# 5. RANKING TARGET POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall maintain rankings for:
- videos
- streams
- broadcasters

# ============================================================
# 6. RANKING PERIOD POLICY
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall maintain at least:
- daily rankings
- monthly rankings
- yearly rankings
- overall rankings

These period scopes shall remain distinct.

# ============================================================
# 7. CANONICAL FIXED STATEMENT
# ============================================================

StreamingOS shall preserve native audience reactions
and native rankings
as internal platform features.

Reaction events and ranking results
shall remain distinct meanings.
