# ============================================================
# INTERNATIONAL OVERVIEW ARCHITECTURE
# ============================================================

status: canonical
layer: architecture

definition:
International overview interface presents diplomacy, alliances,
war state, and international risk in role-appropriate form.

responsibilities:
- display nation relations
- show alliance structure and obligations
- surface war and crisis states
- provide authorized diplomatic or war-related action entry points

rules:
- interface may not reveal unauthorized hidden intelligence
- interface must respect actor authority and visibility scope
- diplomacy and war actions must be explicit and confirmed

final_rule:
International interface must present strategic context clearly
without bypassing authority or visibility rules.
