# OS RESPONSIBILITY BOUNDARY LEDGER

## 1. purpose
This ledger fixes ownership and non-ownership boundaries for each root domain.

---

## 2. 01.civilization-os
### owns
- civilization-wide common operating meaning
- common cross-OS rules
- common identity linkage direction
- shared upper architecture direction

### does not own
- persona inner rendering specifics
- company operational front details
- real-world personal life records
- game runtime specifics
- streaming platform specifics
- static visual/publishing asset runtime specifics
- app-specific UI/product flows

### adjacent boundaries
- with PersonaOS: persona identity/expression boundary
- with BusinessOS: business operation / company usage boundary
- with LifeOS: real-world life support boundary
- with Portal Site: official entry and navigation boundary
- with StaticArtOS: civilization common meaning vs static-asset domain boundary

---

## 3. 02.persona-os
### owns
- persona identity meaning
- persona display/presentation semantics
- persona interaction style
- persona/secretary experience boundary

### does not own
- company master operations
- ERP formal business authority
- user's real-world life master records
- portal/public site authority
- static art asset canonical authority

### adjacent boundaries
- with CivilizationOS: common civilization rules
- with BusinessOS: business-use persona rendering/application boundary
- with LifeOS: user-life support persona usage boundary
- with StaticArtOS: persona presentation use around static-art experiences when applicable

---

## 4. 03.business-os
### owns
- business application operation base
- company-side share/sync/send boundaries
- distributed business app operating common components
- business AI worker / business-side execution support boundaries

### does not own
- ERP as final formal enterprise master for all domains
- persona core identity meaning
- private real-world life truth
- public portal authority
- static art asset master ownership

### adjacent boundaries
- with PersonaOS: business-use persona boundary
- with CivilizationOS: common upper rules boundary
- with 07.applications: concrete business app delivery boundary
- with StaticArtOS: sales/commercial/integration boundary when needed

---

## 5. 04.life-os
### owns
- real-world life support domain
- personal records/plans/support applications
- health / legal / inheritance / life planning support boundaries

### does not own
- civilization-internal fictional life event authority
- company-side formal business operation authority
- portal/public entry authority
- static art asset canonical authority

### adjacent boundaries
- with PersonaOS: life support persona presentation boundary
- with CivilizationOS: common upper rules boundary
- with 07.applications: concrete life application delivery boundary
- with StaticArtOS: user library / reading / viewing adjacency when needed

---

## 6. 05.game-os
### owns
- game-world runtime
- game-side rules and operating structures
- game-specific experience boundaries

### does not own
- real-world life master
- company-side business master
- portal/public entry authority
- static art asset canonical authority

### adjacent boundaries
- with CivilizationOS: civilization-wide common boundary
- with PersonaOS: persona use within game experience when applicable
- with StaticArtOS: static assets as downstream or referenced content when applicable

---

## 7. 06.streaming-os
### owns
- streaming platform operation
- creator/channel/content/distribution boundaries
- platform social interaction / donation boundaries

### does not own
- ERP financial formal master
- real-world life support master
- portal/public entry authority
- static art asset canonical authority

### adjacent boundaries
- with CivilizationOS: civilization identity and upper common rules
- with BusinessOS: settlement/integration boundary when needed
- with PersonaOS: streamer/AI persona presentation boundary
- with StaticArtOS: static-work discovery/promotion/reference boundary when needed

---

## 8. 07.applications
### owns
- concrete app products
- app-specific workflows
- product-specific screens and flows
- app-level feature packaging

### does not own
- upper canonical cross-app truth that belongs to OS layers
- public portal authority unless the app itself is the portal product
- static art canonical asset/rights/entitlement truth

### adjacent boundaries
- receives canonical meaning from neighboring OS layers
- should not redefine upper truth silently

---

## 9. 08.civilization-portal-site
### owns
- official web/public entry
- official navigation across civilization domains
- presentation of official routes and linked destinations
- official access gateway meaning

### does not own
- canonical truth of PersonaOS / BusinessOS / LifeOS / GameOS / StreamingOS / StaticArtOS
- app-internal workflows as their final authority
- replacement of CivilizationOS common truth

### adjacent boundaries
- with CivilizationOS: official entry / navigation / common identity route boundary
- with all OS/app layers: entry, discoverability, public presentation, access routing

---

## 10. 10.staticart-os
### owns
- canonical asset management for static visual works and publishing works
- creator-side upload, metadata, rights, and release management
- marketplace discovery and product listing
- purchase and subscription entitlement reflection
- owned library and shelf experience
- Kindle-like reading continuity for publishing assets
- viewer continuity for visual assets
- projection eligibility source for Exhibition Builder

### does not own
- exhibition placement canonical state
- exhibition route or venue design
- live streaming
- video streaming platform behavior
- city or building design
- portal/public entry authority

### adjacent boundaries
- with CivilizationOS: common upper rules and cross-domain meaning
- with Applications: concrete app realization boundary
- with Portal Site: public discovery and official route boundary
- with BusinessOS: commercial/integration boundary when needed
- with StreamingOS: showcase/reference boundary when needed
