### 3-3. Triple Secret Layer
Stores:

- route-isolated
- policy-isolated

## 5. Normal Route
Normal access is based on:
- caller_code
- requested_scope
- route_policy

This route is for:
- portal
- support apps
- study apps
- consultation apps
- normal reference consumption

## 6. Triple Route
Triple route is a first-class privileged lane.

It is based on:
- access_route = triple
- privilege_tier
- secret eligibility
- secret target selector
- privileged policy semantics

It is not:
- a stronger version of normal route only
- a hidden extension of caller/scope routing
