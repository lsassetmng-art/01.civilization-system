## 3. Priority Reasoning
### 3-1. Phase 1 first
Phase 1 already has the strongest real reuse surface:
- BodyMetrics
- MealPlanner
- TrainingCoach adjacency

### 3-2. Phase 2 next
Exercise and lifestyle are natural extensions of the health/nutrition foundation.

### 3-3. Phase 3 after
Legal/inheritance/end-of-life/life-event are important but less coupled to current phase1 logic.

### 3-4. Phase 4 after that
CareerLaunch is important, but its reusable template layer should be separated from operational workspace data.

## 4. Conclusion
The recommended order is:
- stabilize phase1
- deepen phase2
- broaden into phase3
- add career reusable layer in phase4
