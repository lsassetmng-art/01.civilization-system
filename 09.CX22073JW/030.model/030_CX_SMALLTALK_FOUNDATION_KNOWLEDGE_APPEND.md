## 1. Canonical boundary

CX22073JW is not the canonical owner of AI worker catalogs.
CX22073JW does not own AI worker behavior control.

CX22073JW owns reusable knowledge and reference materials.

For smalltalk, CX22073JW owns:
- reusable smalltalk knowledge
- light topic references
- topic materials
- season / weather / food / hobby / entertainment / daily-life materials

## 2. Canonical CX tables

Neutral table names:
- cx22073jw.knowledge_entry
- cx22073jw.topic_reference
- cx22073jw.topic_material

These names are intentionally neutral.
They are not named after AI workers or a specific series.

## 3. Canonical CX views

Neutral reusable views:
- cx22073jw.vw_knowledge_directory
- cx22073jw.vw_topic_directory
- cx22073jw.vw_topic_material_catalog
- cx22073jw.vw_topic_material_summary

These views expose reusable knowledge/materials.
They do not decide how a worker speaks.

## 5. What CX must not store

CX22073JW must not store as canonical:
- AI worker catalog
- model-service assignment
- worker conversation profile
- forbidden/caution word control
- prohibited topic control
- redirect behavior
- safety boundary behavior
- governance package metadata
- growth delta
- runtime conversation log

Those belong to AIWorkerOS.
