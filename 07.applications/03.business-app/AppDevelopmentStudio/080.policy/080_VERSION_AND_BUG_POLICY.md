# VERSION AND BUG POLICY
# ============================================================

status: canonical-draft
layer: policy
application: App Development Studio

version_policy_principles:
- versioning mode is user-configurable
- artifacts may be version-linked
- bugfix actions may trigger new version assignment

bug_policy_principles:
- bug list should remain visible and filterable
- affected artifacts should be linkable
- retest and closure should be traceable
