## 1. Canon

The following are company-level canon:

- company regulations
- company rules
- design/development rules
- PROJECT_GUARDRAILS-style rules
- quality standards
- security standards
- prohibited actions
- delivery standards
- one-block output rules

## 2. Department Boundary

Departments do not own shared company-wide design/development canon.

Departments own:

- department-specific execution rules
- department-specific review perspectives
- department design document ledger
- department inbox
- task execution records

## 3. Work Packet Inheritance

When a work packet is distributed to a department, company rules and design/development rules are automatically included as inherited context.
