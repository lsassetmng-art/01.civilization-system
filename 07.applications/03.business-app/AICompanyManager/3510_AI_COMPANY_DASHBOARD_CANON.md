# ============================================================
# AICompanyManager AI Company Dashboard Canon
# ============================================================

phase: Phase AL
status: ai-company-dashboard-canon-created

## 1. Canonical Screen Name

Use:

- AI企業ダッシュボード

Do not use:

- 会社ダッシュボード

## 2. Top Page Rule

AI企業ダッシュボード is the default top page when the app opens.

## 3. Company Selection Rule

AI企業ダッシュボード must allow company selection.

Reason:
- user may operate multiple AI companies
- dashboard context must be switchable without going back to settings

## 4. Settings Boundary

AI企業設定 is still responsible for:

- company add
- company change
- company delete
- company common rules

AI企業ダッシュボード is responsible for:

- company overview
- overall status
- department list
- department detail
- organization tree
- operation entrances
