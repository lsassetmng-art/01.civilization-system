## 1. Canon

AICompanyManager is treated as a system-use / internal operation application for now.

Do not design pricing, subscription, paid plan, entitlement, or billing UI for the current scope.

## 2. Current Rule

Current scope:
- system use
- no billing
- no pricing
- no plan selection
- no subscription copy

## 3. Future

If commercialization is needed later, pricing must be designed separately.

Do not mix pricing into the current AICompanyManager operational UI.
