TC-HELP-0001:
  title_en: "Getting started with TrainingCoach"
  summary_en: "A quick guide from your first setup to your first plan."
  body_en: |
    TrainingCoach is an app designed to help you continue exercise in a way that feels manageable.
    At the beginning, you choose a goal, a comfortable level, the time you can spend, and the days that fit your routine.
    Based on that, the app can suggest a starting plan.
    If you are unsure, starting shorter and lighter is usually the best option.
    This app does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.
    If you are not feeling well, prioritize rest over continuing.

TC-HELP-0002:
  title_en: "How to create and adjust your plan"
  summary_en: "You can review your goal, duration, difficulty, and rest days later."
  body_en: |
    A plan is created from a combination of your goal, available time, difficulty, training days, and rest days.
    Your first plan is not fixed forever.
    If it feels hard to continue, you can shorten the duration, lower the difficulty, or increase rest days.
    It is better to optimize for consistency than to make the plan too ambitious.

TC-HELP-0003:
  title_en: "Starting today’s session"
  summary_en: "This article explains the flow from today’s plan to session completion."
  body_en: |
    You can check today’s planned session from the today plan screen.
    When you start a session, you can move through the exercises with simple guidance.
    If you stop in the middle, the session can still be recorded based on its state.
    You do not need to force yourself to continue.
    Stopping and adjusting may be the better choice on some days.

TC-HELP-0004:
  title_en: "Understanding streak and progress"
  summary_en: "A simple guide to how continuity and weekly progress are shown."
  body_en: |
    Your streak is an indicator of how many days you have continued in a row.
    The progress view helps you check your current continuity and weekly completion trend.
    It is helpful to look at these numbers, but not to focus on them so much that you ignore how manageable the routine feels.

TC-HELP-0005:
  title_en: "Reviewing your notification settings"
  summary_en: "You can change reminders if they feel too frequent or not helpful."
  body_en: |
    Notifications are meant to make sessions easier to remember.
    If they feel too frequent, you can reduce them or keep only the ones that help.
    In addition to the app settings, please also check your device-level notification settings.

TC-HELP-0006:
  title_en: "The difference between Free and Premium"
  summary_en: "Core use stays available on Free, while Premium strengthens support and linkage."
  body_en: |
    Free includes core planning, session tracking, and continuity support.
    Premium adds stronger continuity features such as BodyMetrics linkage, condition-based adjustment, and enhanced guidance support.
    Both plans remain non-medical in scope.

TC-HELP-0007:
  title_en: "How to use the app on a low-energy day"
  summary_en: "A safety-first explanation for days when you do not feel well."
  body_en: |
    If you are not feeling well, please prioritize rest.
    If you have pain or strong discomfort, consider stopping instead of continuing.
    If your symptoms continue, consider seeking medical advice.
    It is okay to choose safety over continuity.

TC-HELP-0008:
  title_en: "How data storage and linkage are designed"
  summary_en: "An overview of what data is used and how summaries are shared."
  body_en: |
    TrainingCoach data is designed to live in the LifeOS life domain.
    Linked data from systems such as BodyMetrics is used only within the necessary scope.
    When information is shared with apps such as MealPlanner or LifePlanner, the design is centered on summaries rather than raw full data.

TC-HELP-0009:
  title_en: "What to do if BodyMetrics is not reflected"
  summary_en: "A simple order of checks when linked data does not appear."
  body_en: |
    Linked BodyMetrics data may take some time to appear.
    First, check the sync state on the BodyMetrics side.
    Then reopen TrainingCoach and check again after a short wait.
    If the data still does not appear, try again later before assuming it has failed.

TC-HELP-0010:
  title_en: "What to do if the guide is not showing"
  summary_en: "Basic checks when guidance content does not appear."
  body_en: |
    Guide content may not appear immediately depending on connectivity or content availability.
    Try reopening the screen or checking again later.
    In some cases, locale differences or missing content availability may also affect what you see.
