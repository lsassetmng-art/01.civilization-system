TC_FAQ_0001_Q:
  en: "What kind of app is TrainingCoach?"

TC_FAQ_0001_A:
  en: "TrainingCoach helps with workout planning, session tracking, and continuity support. It is meant to support everyday exercise habits and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment."

TC_FAQ_0002_Q:
  en: "Can I change the first plan later?"

TC_FAQ_0002_A:
  en: "Yes. You can adjust your goal, difficulty, time, and schedule later. If you are unsure, it is usually best to start short and light."

TC_FAQ_0003_Q:
  en: "What happens if I stop a session halfway?"

TC_FAQ_0003_A:
  en: "The session can still be recorded based on its state. Some days, stopping and adjusting is better than pushing through."

TC_FAQ_0004_Q:
  en: "How is my streak calculated?"

TC_FAQ_0004_A:
  en: "Your streak is based on consecutive days of completed activity. Depending on the session result, some incomplete or skipped sessions may not extend the streak."

TC_FAQ_0005_Q:
  en: "Can I turn off or change notifications?"

TC_FAQ_0005_A:
  en: "Yes. You can review notification settings inside the app. If reminders still do not behave as expected, also check your device-level notification settings."

TC_FAQ_0006_Q:
  en: "What do I get with Premium?"

TC_FAQ_0006_A:
  en: "Premium adds features such as BodyMetrics linkage, condition-based adjustments, stronger progress visibility, and enhanced guidance support. Core planning and tracking stay available on Free."

TC_FAQ_0007_Q:
  en: "What should I do if I do not feel well?"

TC_FAQ_0007_A:
  en: "Please prioritize rest. If you have pain or strong discomfort, consider stopping. If symptoms continue, consider seeking medical advice."

TC_FAQ_0008_Q:
  en: "Where is my data stored?"

TC_FAQ_0008_A:
  en: "TrainingCoach data is designed as LifeOS life-domain data. Linked data is used only within the needed scope, and outbound sharing is designed around summaries rather than full raw data."

TC_FAQ_0009_Q:
  en: "Why is my BodyMetrics data not showing up?"

TC_FAQ_0009_A:
  en: "There may be a delay before linked data appears. First check the sync status on the BodyMetrics side, then reopen TrainingCoach and try again after a short wait."

TC_FAQ_0010_Q:
  en: "Why is the guide not showing?"

TC_FAQ_0010_A:
  en: "Guide content may not appear immediately depending on connectivity or content availability. Try reopening the screen or checking again later."
