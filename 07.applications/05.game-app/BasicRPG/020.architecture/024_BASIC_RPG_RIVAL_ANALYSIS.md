comparison_targets:
- AFK Journey
- Genshin Impact
- Honkai: Star Rail

takeaways:
AFK Journey shows the value of lightweight progression, storybook readability,
and strategy-card combat with mobile-friendly session design.
Genshin Impact shows the pull of world exploration, audiovisual polish,
and broad cross-platform reach.
Honkai: Star Rail shows the accessibility of turn-based combat,
strong character presentation, and recurring pass + premium currency structure.

recommended_differentiation:
- smaller scope but clearer progression
- lighter asset burden than open-world RPGs
- turn-based readability for mobile and PC alike
- fair monetization without loot-box dependency in v1
- stronger offline tolerance and resume continuity
- lower operational burden by avoiding PvP/chat-first scope in v1
