product_definition:
BasicRPG is a chapter-based, turn-based fantasy RPG application.

v1_definition:
- player controls one account profile
- player forms a small party from owned heroes
- player advances through chapters, quests, and dungeons
- combat is turn-based and menu-readable
- gear, leveling, and skill growth are core progression
- purchases focus on convenience, cosmetics, pass, and optional bundles
- app supports iPhone, Android, PC, and tablet
- app supports Japanese and English first
- app supports multicurrency pricing display and billing mapping

non_v1_scope:
- PvP arena
- guild raid
- real-time co-op battle
- user-to-user trading
- open market economy
- gacha-dependent design
