Defines policy requirements of StreamStudio.

# 1. RIGHTS CHECK POLICY

Before publication or sale, StreamStudio must support checks for:
- source ownership basis
- collaborator contribution basis
- thumbnail rights
- subtitle rights
- destination eligibility
- marketplace eligibility
- blocked internal-only material exclusion
- membership-only release rights compatibility

# 2. APPROVAL POLICY

Approval must remain explicit when:
- role policy requires approval
- rights risk exists
- publication is scheduled to external destination
- marketplace listing enters paid mode
- membership program becomes active
- split conflict exists
- policy override was requested

# 3. PRICING POLICY

- one-time price must be positive
- currency must be explicit
- free mode and paid mode must not coexist ambiguously on the same offer
- sale window must be valid
- membership-only mode must not silently inherit one-time purchase mode

# 4. MEMBERSHIP POLICY

- membership program activation may require review
- tier order must be unique
- benefit visibility must be explicit
- members-only content rules must be auditable
- early access expiration must be explicit
- program pause and end must preserve history

# 5. SPLIT POLICY

- percentage total must remain valid
- fixed fee currency must be explicit
- negative payout is forbidden
- overlapping active split versions are forbidden
- approval may be required for split activation
- paid listing may require split configuration before listing activation

# 6. PRIVACY POLICY

- private and limited drafts must not leak through search
- collaborator access must be scope-restricted
- negotiation logs may require reduced visibility
- member reference views must follow minimum necessary visibility
- connector credentials must remain isolated

# 7. POLICY FIXED STATEMENT

StreamStudio shall preserve explicit rights checks,
explicit approval boundaries,
auditable pricing and membership governance,
and auditable split governance
before and after publication or sale actions.
