## 1. Definition

StreamWatch is the official viewer-front application of StreamingOS.
It is the primary application for watching video assets, live streams, archived streams, clips, series, and playlists through structured discovery, viewer continuity, and TV-connected playback.

## 2. Mission

The mission of StreamWatch is to let a viewer discover, enter, resume, and continue content with low friction while preserving profile-aware continuity, entitlement-aware watchability, and large-screen handoff behavior.

## 3. Fixed Boundaries

StreamWatch owns:

- viewer-facing playback entry
- structured discovery
- category-tree browsing
- follow, favorites, watch-later, history, and library UX
- profile-aware continuity
- restriction gate UX
- purchase, rental, and membership execution surfaces
- TV route handoff UX

StreamWatch does not own:

- creator editing
- content upload operation
- publication workflow
- revenue split control
- creator collaboration workflow

## 4. Cross-System Rule

Civilization and StreamingOS may both provide purchase, rental, and membership entry surfaces.
The execution backbone is shared commerce logic, while StreamingOS remains the canonical owner of playback entitlement, playback eligibility, and archive availability.

## 5. Directory Intent

This folder stores the full implementation-ready design set for StreamWatch using the same style as the broader Civilization-system application structure.
