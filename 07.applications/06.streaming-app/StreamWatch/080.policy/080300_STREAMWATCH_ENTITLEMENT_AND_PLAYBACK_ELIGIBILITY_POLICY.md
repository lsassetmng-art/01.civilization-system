## 1. Purpose

This document defines the policy relationship between commercial access rights and actual playback eligibility inside StreamWatch.

## 2. Fixed Principle

Commercial success alone does not directly cause file delivery.
Commercial success causes entitlement grant, and StreamWatch evaluates playback eligibility from the resulting StreamingOS state.

## 3. Eligibility Order

1. content existence and publication state
2. entitlement existence
3. regional or distribution constraints
4. viewer_profile restriction result
5. device or session constraints

## 4. CTA Consequences

Depending on state, the viewer may see:

- play_now
- resume
- watch_live
- watch_archive
- buy_now
- rent_now
- join_membership
- preorder
- unavailable

## 5. Important Rule

Entitlement is necessary but not always sufficient.
A purchased or entitled asset may still be blocked by restriction mode, time window, or route/device limitations.
