# APPLICATIONS OVERVIEW

## role
`07.applications` is the concrete product/application container
under `01.civilization-system`.

It is not an OS.
It is the place where user-facing apps are organized and delivered.

## primary responsibilities
- app packaging
- concrete app UX
- app-specific screen flow
- app-local drafts/cache/offline preparation
- concrete product realization

## non-responsibilities
- not the canonical owner of civilization common meaning
- not the canonical owner of persona identity meaning
- not the canonical owner of business operating meaning
- not the canonical owner of life support meaning
- not the canonical owner of game runtime meaning
- not the canonical owner of streaming platform meaning
- not the official public portal owner

## boundary summary
Applications consume upper meanings from OS layers
and turn them into concrete products.
