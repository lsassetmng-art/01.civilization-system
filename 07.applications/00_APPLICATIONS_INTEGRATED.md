# APPLICATIONS INTEGRATED

## summary
`07.applications` is the product realization layer
inside `01.civilization-system`.

## fixed interpretation
- type: application-container
- not an OS
- not the official portal
- receives canonical meanings from OS layers
- turns them into concrete app experiences

## allowed ownership
Applications may own:
- app-only UX structure
- app-local drafts
- local cache
- app packaging
- app-specific feature composition

## disallowed ownership
Applications may not own:
- civilization-wide common meaning
- persona canonical identity meaning
- business canonical operating meaning
- life canonical support meaning
- game canonical authority
- streaming canonical platform authority
- official public portal authority

## relation to portal
Portal is the official public entry layer.
Applications are destinations and concrete products.

## relation to OS layers
Applications should implement and realize,
not replace and redefine.
